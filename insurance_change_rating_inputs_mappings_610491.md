# Change Rating Inputs Mappings

Change the way rating inputs are mapped to attributes as needed.

1.  Go back to the coverage spec, insured item spec, or insured party spec detail page.
2.  Click the input value to open a dropdown with available inputs.
3.  Choose the one you want to change this mapping to.
