# Create an Agent from the Agentforce Insurance Service Assistance Template

Use the Insurance Service Assistance template of type Agentforce Employee Agent to get an agent up and running for your insurance reps. You can customize the template to include topics and actions specific to your business needs.

1.  From Setup, enter **Agents**, and then select **Agentforce Agents**.
2.  Click **New Agent**.
3.  Select the **Insurance Service Assistance** template, and then click **Next**.
4.  Review the topic, Insurance Summarization, that comes with your template, and then click **Next**.
5.  Customize your agent, enter a company name, and then click **Next**.
6.  Select a language and tone, then click **Create**.
7.  From the action menu for the new agent, click **Open in Builder**.
8.  In Agentforce Builder, use the Conversation Preview area to test your agent with some utterances.
9.  Click **Activate** to activate the agent.
