# Create an Insurance Policyholder Portal

Work with a Financial Services Experience Builder template to create a self-service portal for your customers. Perform initial setup and branding, configure components, and then deploy a working portal to your sandbox.

From the Financial Services site template, configure pre-built components that allow policyholders to:

-   View policy and claim summaries.
    
-   Drill down to view details of policies and claim records.
    
-   Upload relevant documents for claims.
    

Policyholders can launch any of these pages from the portal home page.

Note The Insurance Policyholder Portal follows standard Experience Workspaces conventions:

-   Use Experience Builder to configure, brand, and customize the Home page and pre-built components.
    
-   Use Content Management and Moderation to set up the Knowledge Base.
    
-   Use the [checklist](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_experience_cloud_site_setup_checklist__for_fsc_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Use this checklist as a guide to perform Experience Cloud site setup tasks, to brand the home page and to update component properties.") as a guide to perform Experience Cloud site setup tasks, to brand the home page, and to update component properties.
    
-   [Work with the home page](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.fsc_admin_portal_customize_home_page.htm&language=en_US&type=5) to personalize your Experience Cloud site.
