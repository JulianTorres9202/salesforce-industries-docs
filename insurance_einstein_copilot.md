# Einstein Generative AI for Insurance

Bring the power of generative AI to your insurance business with Einstein.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Note

This feature uses [Einstein Generative AI](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.generative_ai_about.htm&language=en_US&type=5) and consumes Einstein requests. Einstein Generative AI features are provided on a separate infrastructure with different security protections and physical hosting locations than the services. See [Salesforce Trust and Compliance Documentation](https://www.salesforce.com/company/legal/trust-and-compliance-documentation/) for more information.

-   **[Einstein Copilot for Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_einstein_copilot_for_fsc_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Enhance your customer engagements by streamlining business tasks by using the power of conversational AI using Einstein Copilot. With the Einstein Copilot for Insurance, customer service representatives (CSR) can gain quick insights by using AI-generated summaries of Account, Insurance Policy, and Claim records.
-   **[Einstein Summary for Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_einstein_summary_for_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Empower your customer service representatives (CSRs) or captive agents to quickly generate a summary of the information stored in Account or Claim records by using Einstein Summary. The summary provides key details such as the policy name and type, standard premium, coverage details, or claim details. This feature improves operational efficiency and reduces response times. As a result, customer satisfaction increases, leading to better business outcomes.
-   **[Record Summary Prompt Templates for Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_record_summary_prompt_templates_for_fsc_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    To generate summaries of Account, Insurance Policy, and Claim records, the Einstein Copilot for Insurance or Einstein Summary for Insurance uses record summary prompt templates.
