# Summarize Insurance Policyholders and Get Quotes

Use the Agentforce for Financial Services: Insurance Service Assistance agent to summarize policyholder portfolios and get individual insurance quotes.

2.  Ask the agent questions such as these:
    
    Summarize policyholder John Doe.
    
    Give me details about a quote named Auto Gold Annual Insurance Quote.
    
    Show me the details of account John Doe.
    
    Retrieve the details for this quote named Auto Gold Annual Insurance Quote.
