# Policyholder Digital Experience Site Policies Page

Your Policyholder site users have a policy record page for each of their policies.

Out of the box, the Policies page looks like this:

On this page, your users can:

-   See an overview of the policy
    
-   View open claims for the policy
    
    If open claims exist, the claim name is a link to the claim record page
    
-   View recent transaction for the policy
    
-   See details about insured items or parties
    
-   Update policy information
    

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Note

Remember, you must customize these components for your own Policyholder site.

Note

The Policies FSC page mimics the look and functionality of the Policies page but uses a Policy Details component designed for FSC policies.
