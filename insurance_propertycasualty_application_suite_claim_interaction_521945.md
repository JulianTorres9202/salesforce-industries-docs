# Insurance Property & Casualty Application Suite Claim Interaction

A service agent can verify claim details with the interaction launcher.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)After receiving a call:

1.  Open the Interaction Launcher from the utility bar at the bottom.
2.  In the **CLAIM SEARCH** section, enter the claim number and click **Search**.
3.  The results are displayed and you need to click **Verify** to corroborate customer information.
4.  Based on the caller's answers, click the checkmark to verify relevant details and then click **Launch Console** to view complete claim information.
