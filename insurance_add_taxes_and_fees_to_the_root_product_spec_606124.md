# Add Taxes and Fees to the Root Product Spec

To apply taxes and fees to insurance product premiums, you can attach them to root products.

1.  On the **Taxes and Fees** tab, click **Add**.
2.  Select one or more taxes and fees, then click **Select**.
3.  On each tax and fee, make any changes to the tax or fee if needed.
    
    Click each tax or fee name to open it in the right panel.
    
    Important
    
    If you make a change to the tax or fee here on the root product spec, that change gets inherited by the original tax or fee record.
    
    This change is automatically pushed to any other root product specs and child product specs that have this tax or fee attached.
    
4.  Click **Update** to save changes you made to the tax or fee.
