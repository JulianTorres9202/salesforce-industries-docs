## Required Add-Ons

The products and add-on licenses in your Salesforce org determine whether you have the necessary flows and prompts to use Einstein generative AI. To use Einstein generative AI, you need these add-ons:

-   Einstein GPT Platform
    
-   Einstein for Service Innovations
    
-   FSCServiceEinstein
    

## Required Permissions

Einstein Summary works with Einstein GPT and Prompt Builder. So, you must assign these permission sets to your users:

-   **Summarize Record Details:** Assign this permission to the users who use Einstein Summary.
    
-   **Einstein GPT Prompt Template User:** Assign this permission to the users who use Prompt Builder.
    
-   **Industry Service Excellence:** Assign this permission set to the users who use the Service Innovations Einstein: Get Engagement Interaction Details flow, Generate Engagement Interaction Summary prompt template, and Get Record Details invocable action.
    

To see what's included in a permission set, click **View Summary** on the permission set's detail page in Setup.
