# Insurance View Policy Permission Set

Give your agents access to just what they need to view insurance policies.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Access Provided by the Insurance View Policy Permission Set

This permission set is delivered in the Insurance Industries Extension managed package and provides read-only access to the following objects:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

-   Account
    
-   Attribute Assignment
    
-   Compiled Attribute Override
    
-   Customer Property
    
-   Insurance Policy
    
-   Insurance Policy Asset
    
-   Insurance Policy Coverage
    
-   Insurance Policy Member Asset
    
-   Insurance Policy Participant
    
-   Insurance Policy Payment Schedule Entry
    
-   Insurance Policy Revenue Schedule
    
-   Insurance Policy Surcharge
    
-   Insurance Policy Term
    
-   Insurance Policy Transaction
    
-   Pricebook
    
-   Price List
    
-   Price List Entry
    
-   Producer
    
-   Producer Commission
    
-   Product
    
-   Product Child Item
    
-   Product Requirement
    
-   Production Code
    
-   Quote
    

Additionally, this permission set grants read-only access to certain fields on some of the objects listed above.
