# Odds and Ends in Insurance Spring '21

In addition to the big features described above, we've made a few smaller changes to Vlocity Insurance that we want you to know about.

**Where**: Users who have a Vlocity Insurance license can access these enhancements.

**Why**: We've included the following enhancements:

-   Transaction reversals
    
    You can now reverse transactions that have posted on insurance policies. The system calculates the reversal and any reversed surcharges, and creates the transactions needed to amend the insurance policy for the reversal.
    
    To learn more, see [Manage Insurance Policies](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policy_administration_621128.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Insurance Policy Administration extends throughout the lifecycle of an insurance policy. Any updates made to a policy during its term are managed through policy transactions.").
    
-   Trailing document enhancement
    
    The trailing document feature has been updated so it works with the Digital Experience sites
    

**How**: You get these features with the Vlocity Health and Insurance Spring '21 managed package. You don't need to do any additional setup or configuration to use them.
