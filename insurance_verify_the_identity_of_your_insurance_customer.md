# Verify the Identity of Your Insurance Customer

Use the prebuilt **Insurance: Verify Caller Identity** flow to verify the identity of inbound calling insurance members or authorized member representatives based on the details they provide against the existing CRM data.

**Where**: This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package.

**Who**: The flow is available to users who are assigned the Industry Service Excellence permission set.

**How**: In Flow Builder, run the **Insurance: Verify Caller** flow and decide whether to use the flow as is or customize it.
