# Create Claim Product Specifications

With Product Catalog Management, you can define a specification type for the products that are unique to your industry. A product specification type is associated with a product specification record type.

-   **[Create Record Types](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_claim_rec_types.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create Claim, Claim Damage, and Claim Injury record types on the Product2 object.
-   **[Create Product Specification Type](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_claim_prd_spec_type.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create Claim, ClaimDamage, and ClaimInjury product specification types.
-   **[Create Product Specification Record Type](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_claim_prd_spec_rec_type.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create Claim, Claim Damage, and Claim Injury product specification record types.
