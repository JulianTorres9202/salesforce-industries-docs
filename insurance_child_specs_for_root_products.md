# Child Specs for Root Products

Child specs are key building blocks for your product models. They're components of the root product specs that Insurance rates, quotes, sells, and administers for your customers.

-   **[Child Specs in Context](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_child_specs_in_context_605004.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use child specs to create insurance products, health plans, and claim products. Insurance products and health plans can contain one or more child specs. Make sure that part of your planning includes deciding what types of child specs go into your root product specs.
-   **[Workflow for Creating Child Specs with Attributes](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_workflow_for_creating_child_specs_with_attributes_605248.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    After you weigh different options and decide on a plan for child specs, their attributes, and which child specs go with each root product, you're ready to build your child specs.
