# Commercial Auto Product Model

The Commercial Auto product is a single-instance product model that lets businesses create quotes for multiple vehicles that are tied to a single location.

-   **[Insured Items for Commercial Auto Products Reference](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insured_items_for_commercial_auto_products_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Commercial Auto product includes several insured items.
-   **[Coverages for Commercial Auto Products](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_coverages_for_commercial_auto_products_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Coverage specs define the required and optional coverages on an insurance product. On the Product page, the Product Record Type at the top left of the page = Coverage Spec.
-   **[Commercial Auto Root Product](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_commercial_auto_root_product_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The root product spec is the container for the Commercial Auto product model. The system uses the data stored in this product model in downstream business processes, including quoting, and issuing policies.
-   **[Attributes for Commercial Auto Products](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_attributes_for_commercial_auto_products_with_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    All the attributes used in the CommercialAuto insurance product and the insured item spec, insured party spec, and coverage specs are defined in the Vlocity Attribute Designer. Attributes are arranged into Attribute Categories.
-   **[Simulate Rating for Commercial Auto Products](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_simulate_rating_for_commercial_auto_products_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Simulate tab on the root product spec has the ratings set up to simulate rating the Multi Auto product model.
