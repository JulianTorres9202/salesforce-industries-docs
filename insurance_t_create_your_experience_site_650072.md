# Create Your Experience Site

To start using the templates provided in the unmanaged package, create an Experience site for your users.

The highlighted area in this flow shows where you are in the process of setting up your Experience site:

1.  From Setup, enter Digital Experiences in the Quick Find box, then select **Sites**.
2.  Select **New Site**.
3.  Select the Insurance **Broker** or **Policy Holder** template.
4.  On the splash screen, click **Get Started**.
5.  In the **Name** field, enter the name you want your users to see.
6.  In URL, enter the string you want after the slash. Users will see this URL.
7.  Click **Create**.
