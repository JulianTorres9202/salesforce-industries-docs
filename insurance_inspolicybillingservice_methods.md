# InsPolicyBillingService Methods

These are the available InsPolicyBillingService methods.

-   **[InsPolicyBillingService:generateBillingAccountStatements](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_inspolicybillingservice__generatebillingaccountstatements.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use this service to generate statements for accounts that contain policies with billing accounts that have Due Date = today.
-   **[InsPolicyBillingService:generateDirectBillingStatements](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_inspolicybillingservice__generatedirectbillingstatements.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use this service to generate statements for accounts containing policies with direct billing whose due date is equal to today's date plus ten (10) days.
