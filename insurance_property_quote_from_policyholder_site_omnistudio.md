# Property Quote from Policyholder Site

Policyholders can follow a guided flow to quickly create quotes for property insurance.

All our screenshots are currently in Lightning style. Your spin may look different because it's using the Newport style in the Broker Portal and Customer Portal.

1.  From the policyholder site, click **Homeowners/Renters**.
2.  Click **Next**.
3.  Enter details of the home you want to insure and click **Next**.
    
    Tip
    
    To navigate through this flow quickly using seed data, click **autoFill** at the bottom of the form.
    
4.  Enter a few more home details and click **New**.
5.  Enter information and the value of the contents of your home and click **Next**.
6.  Modify any coverages and click **New**.
7.  Verify or change your contact details and click **Next**.
8.  You can download the quote proposal in either Word or PDF format. When you're done, click **Next**.
    
    You receive a confirmation.
    
9.  Click **Done**.
    
    You can see the created quote on your Home page as well.
