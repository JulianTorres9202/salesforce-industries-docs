# Request Insurance Proof Service Process

​​With the Request Insurance Proof service process, your customers can request proof of insurance through a straightforward and simplified process.

-   **[Streamline Requesting Proof Of Insurance by Using Service Process Studio](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_streamline_requesting_poi_using_service_process_studio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use the Service Process Studio to enable the Request Insurance Proof service process to give your agents a simplified process for requesting ‌proof of insurance.
-   **[Submit an Insurance Proof Request](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_submit_an_insurance_proof_request.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Customers can independently request ‌proof of insurance through self-service portals, or service agents can initiate the request for them.
