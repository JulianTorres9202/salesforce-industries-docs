# Insurance Spring '21 Release Notes

These release notes provide details about the changes in the Spring '21 release of Insurance.

-   **[Harmonized Insurance Policy Object Model](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_harmonized_insurance_policy_object_model.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    It's time! You can use the Insurance Policy core object model on Salesforce FSC for all your insurance policy data.
-   **[Broker Digital Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_broker_digital_experience_site.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Your brokers and agents need access to quotes, policies, and accounts they work with to sell insurance products and manage existing policies. Use the Digital Experience template and components to get you started in building a custom Digital Experience site that meets those needs.
-   **[Policyholder Digital Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policyholder_digital_experience_site.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Your policyholders need access to view and make changes to their account, policies, claims, and billing information. The Policyholder Digital Experience template and components get you started in building a custom Digital Experience site that meets those needs.
-   **[Group Voluntary Benefits Application](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_group_voluntary_benefits_application.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Your users can cut down the time they spend on finding the ideal plan and then enrolling employees. We've made intuitive workflows that take out the guesswork with complete guidance for every step. Your service agents can provide better customer service by using the interaction console to quickly get a 360-degree view of the customer.
-   **[Property & Casualty Application Spring '21](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_property___casualty_application.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Your users can now file a FNOL for a multi auto policy using a guided flow. Empower your service agents with a 360-degree view of the customer from the OmniStudio FlexCard-based interaction console.
-   **[Odds and Ends in Insurance Spring '21](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_odds_and_ends.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    In addition to the big features described above, we've made a few smaller changes to Vlocity Insurance that we want you to know about.
-   **[Relationship Graphs](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_relationship_graphs_6115.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Refresh Relationship Graphs programmatically, use additional filter operators, and include one-to-many relationships in Timeline graphs.
-   **[Data Model Updates for Insurance Spring '21](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_data_model_updates_for_insurance_spring__21.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    These are the data model updates for this release.
-   **[Data Model Updates for Insurance FSC Spring '21](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_data_model_updates_for_insurance_fsc_spring21_7131.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    These are the data model updates for this release.
