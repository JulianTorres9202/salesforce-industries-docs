# Add a Coverage

You can add a coverage to a product or insured item.

1.  Click **Show Coverages** on the product or insured item you want to add coverage to.
    
    All of the current coverages and their attributes for a product or insured item display. Any optional coverages available for this quote appear at the bottom of the list.
    
2.  Click **Add** to add one of these coverages to the quote.
    
    The coverage's attributes appear below its name.
    
3.  Make changes to the attributes of the newly added coverage as needed.
