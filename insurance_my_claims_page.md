# My Claims Page

Your policyholders have a list view of the claim records associated with their policy.

On this page, your policyholders can:

-   See a summary of all claims.
    
-   Refine the list view by using a search keyword such as the claim number to filter the claims.
    
-   View a claim record in detail by clicking the claim number.
