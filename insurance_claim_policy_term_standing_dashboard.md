1.  From the App Launcher, find and select **Claims**.
2.  Select a claim from the list view.
3.  Navigate to the Claim Financials tab and open a claim coverage.
4.  A side panel opens, displaying the financial dashboard with two sections:
    -   **Overall Loss Summary:** This summary shows the pending and processed amounts for loss items. Any Claim Coverage Policy Detail (CCPD) record that isn’t marked as Canceled or Paid is considered pending, while a CCPD with a Paid status is considered paid. This information is presented in a donut chart.
    -   **Current Limit Standings:** This section offers a detailed overview of the total limit, remaining balance, consumed amount, and pending amounts for deductibles, copays, limit currency, and out-of-pocket maximums for each scope. The scopes include Claims, Claim Coverage, Policy, and Policy Coverage for a CCPD record. This breakdown helps to clearly understand the financial status and utilization within each specific scope.
