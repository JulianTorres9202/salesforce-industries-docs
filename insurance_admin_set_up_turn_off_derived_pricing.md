# Turn Off Automatic Addition of Derived Pricing Assets

When you add a contributing product to a quote or order, prevent its derived pricing assets from being added automatically.

1.  From Setup, in the Quick Find box, find and select **Revenue Settings**.
2.  In the Quote, Order, and Configuration Settings section, turn off Add Derived Pricing Assets to a Quote or Order.
    
    Your changes are autosaved.
