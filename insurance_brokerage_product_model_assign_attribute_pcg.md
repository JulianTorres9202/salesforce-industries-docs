# Assign the Attribute to the Product Classification

After creating a product classification and attribute, assign the attribute to the product classification. For example, assign the Deductibles attribute to the Annual Deductibles product classification.

Use this procedure to assign an attribute to a product classification.

1.  In the Attributes tab of the Annual Deductibles Product Classification record, click **Assign**.
2.  Select **Assign individual attributes** and click **Next**.
3.  In the Assign Attributes window, select **Deductibles** under the Uncategorized tab, and then click **Assign**.
