# Quickly Delete All Members of the Group Census

Reduce time and effort to delete up to 10,000 members each with up to five plans and up to five coverages selected per plan. Also, you can delete up to 150,000 members without any plans.

**Where**: This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package and the Insurance Industries Extension managed package.

**How**: Use the InsCensusServiceStd:deleteAllMembers​ service in the delete members workflow.
