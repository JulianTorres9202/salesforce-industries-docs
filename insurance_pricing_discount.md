# Pricing Discount for Multiroot Quotes

To calculate discounts and reprice premiums during the issuance and endorsement of multiroot quotes, use the Pricing Discount out-of-the-box solution. Enable the PricingDiscountIntegProcName custom setting to use this solution in the Insurance Commercial Quote Lightning web component.

This solution uses an integration procedure and an expression set or calculation procedure to determine discounts and reprice premiums based on the number of root products added to or removed from a multiroot quote. It reduces manual work, minimizes errors, and ensures accurate discounts, resulting in competitive pricing that enhances customer satisfaction and sales.

-   **[Set Up Pricing Discount for Multiroot Quotes](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_set_up_pricing_discount.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Pricing Discount is an out-of-the-box solution used for calculating discounts on premiums for multiroot quotes. Your users can set up how discounts are calculated and applied to root-level products during the rating process.
-   **[Enable Pricing Discount for a Commercial Quote](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_enable_pricing_discount.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Configure the custom setting that enables ‌pricing discount support in your Lightning web component.
-   **[Calculate Pricing Discount for a Commercial Multiroot Quote](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_calculate_pricing_discount.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Add or remove a product to a commercial quote to calculate the discounted premium for the quote. You can recalculate the premium every time you add or remove products.
