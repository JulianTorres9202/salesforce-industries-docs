# Build Complex Commercial Quotes

New and enhanced Lightning web components help users configure, reprice, and navigate complex commercial quotes with multi-level insured item relationships and coverages.

**Where:** This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package.
