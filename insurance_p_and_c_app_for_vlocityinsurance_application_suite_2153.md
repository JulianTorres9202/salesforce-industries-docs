# Property and Casualty Application for Vlocity Insurance Application Suite

All new commercial products for quoting and rating, enhancements to the policyholder Experience site, new claim recovery process and more.

-   **[Create a Quote from the Policyholder Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_create_a_quote_from_the_policyholder_experience_site_2174.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Policyholders can follow a guided flow on the policyholder Experience Site to quickly create quotes for Auto, Marine, or Property insurance.
-   **[Authorize Claim Payments](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_authorize_claim_payments_2201.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Claim approvers, claim handlers, and claim adjusters are all assigned a maximum limit that they can authorize a claim payment for. If the claim amount is over the authorized limit for a particular role, an approval process is triggered for the claim and it goes to a Claim Manager.
-   **[Initiate Claim Recovery](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_initiate_claim_recovery_2223.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Claims adjusters have the responsibility of deciding how much money can be given for each claim. With the Claim Adjuster App, users can now quickly create a recovery record from the insurance claim.
-   **[Workers Compensation Product for Commercial Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_workers_compensation_product_for_commercial_insurance_2245.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Commercial line of business now comes with a fully functional example of the Workers Compensation product models, rating procedures, and business processes for quoting.
