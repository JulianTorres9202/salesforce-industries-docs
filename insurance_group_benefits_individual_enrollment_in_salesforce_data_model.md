# Group Benefits Individual Enrollment in the Salesforce Data Model

Customers can view and select eligible plans with optional coverages, and then rate the plans and enroll. Customers can also see the member level premium along with employee and employer contributions for a policy before enrollment. Customers can enroll right away or save the selections for enrolling later.

-   **[Configure Group Benefits Individual Enrollment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_configure_group_benefits_individual_enrollment.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create a workflow that gives policy holders a straightforward way to view and select eligible plans, and then rate the plans and enroll.
-   **[Get to Know the Workflow for Group Benefits Individual Enrollment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_get_to_know_the_workflow_for_group_benefits_individual_enrollment.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Give group members a straightforward way for self enrollment into selected plans from the customer portal.
