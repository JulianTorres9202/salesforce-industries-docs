# Bulk Enrollment for Insurance Application Suite

New products that offer additional plans to employees, a brand new user interface for the policyholder enrollment business process, and a new bulk enrollment process that lets employers enroll up to 500 members at once.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**Where:** This feature is available in the Insurance Application Suite under Group Voluntary Benefits Application.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**Why:** Provide employees with a better user experience with the new user interface as they complete enrollment and also offer more plan options for covering employees and their family. Employers can now enjoy a simplified enrollment process by adding up to 500 members in bulk.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**How:** This feature is auto-enabled in the Group Voluntary Benefits Application Application Suite org that [you can request](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs "Insurance business apps are pre-built solutions created with industry experts to help you modernize your business and focus more on your customers. These apps are delivered through reference orgs that include the Insurance managed package. To accelerate your digital transformation, these reference orgs also have sample configuration and seed data you can migrate into your production org. Industries Business App reference orgs are only available to current customers and partners.").

The new user interface uses configurable FlexCards to provide an enhanced user experience to employees as they enroll themselves. Employees now also have the option to select a Term Life plan which includes Supplemental Life and Spouse Life as optional coverages during enrollment.

Employers can now enroll up to 500 members during enrollment. This enhanced flow is based on an OmniStudio OmniScript that you can find under:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

-   Type/Subtype - InsEnrollment/Bulk
    
-   OmniScript Name - Bulk Enrollment
