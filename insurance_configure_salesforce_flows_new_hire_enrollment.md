# Configure the Salesforce Flow for New Hire Enrollment

The Enrollment flow compiles available products for group census members and process bulk enrollment.

To configure the Enrollment Flow for new hire enrollment, see [Configure the Salesforce Flow for Enrollment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_configure_the_salesforce_flow_for_individual_enrollment.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Use this flow to enable group census members to enroll themselves and their dependents into the selected plans.").
