# Add the History Tab to Claim Records

Add the History tab to records to make it available to users.

1.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)On a claim, click the quick access menu (the gear icon) and select **Edit Page**.
2.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Click the Tabs component, then click **Add Tab**.
3.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Click the new tab and enter label values.
    
    | 
    **Tab Label**
    
     | 
    
    Custom
    
     |
    | --- | --- |
    | 
    
    **Custom Label**
    
     | 
    
    History
    
     |
    
4.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Find the **Insurance History** component, then drag it to the new History tab.
