# Digital Insurance Learning Journeys

Learning journeys guide you to the best learning content to get you skilled up and ready to succeed in your implementation projects. Learning journeys are organized by cloud and role, and include a mix of Trailhead modules, projects, trails, trailmixes, superbadges, and certifications as well as Trailhead Academy classes.

-   **[Digital Insurance Platform Consultant Learning Journey](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_consultant_learning_journey.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_consultant_learning_journey)**  
    This learning journey is for anyone in a business role with a focus in the insurance industries. The journey will guide you through all of the Salesforce Industries product training courses and certifications that are needed to prepare you to consult on a Digital Insurance Platform implementation project.
-   **[Digital Insurance Platform Developer Learning Journey](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_developer_learning_journey.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_developer_learning_journey)**  
    This learning journey is for anyone in a technical role with a focus in the insurance industries. The journey will guide you through all of the Salesforce Industries product training courses and certifications that are needed to prepare you to work on a Digital Insurance Platform implementation project.
