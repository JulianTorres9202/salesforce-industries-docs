# Add the Product Attribute Processing Definition Component to the Product Details Page

Use Lightning App Builder to add the Product Attribute Processing Definition Lightning web component to the policy root product details page.

1.  From the App Launcher, find and select **Product Catalog Management**.
2.  Select a policy root product.
3.  On the root product details page, from Setup, select **Edit Page**.
4.  To add a tab, click **Add Tab** in the Tabs component properties.
5.  Customize a tab by clicking it in the properties pane. You can select a different standard label, or click **Custom** and enter the tab name you want.
6.  From the Components pane to the left, in the **Search** field, enter Product Attribute Processing Definition.
7.  To add the component to a tab, select the tab on the canvas, and then drop the component directly below it.
8.  Click **Save**.
9.  Click **Activation**.
10.  In the Activation window that opens, to choose whether you want to Assign or Remove your org as default, follow the prompts. You can also leave it as is.
11.  Save your changes.
12.  To exit the Lightning App Builder and return to your Record page, click the back arrow.
