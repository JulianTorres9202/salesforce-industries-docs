## Create an Insurance Quote

Discover and price insurance products for your customers by using quotes.

### Required Editions

| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To create quotes: | Create on Quotes |

1.  Create a quote.
    -   On an opportunity, in the Quotes section, click **New Quote**.
    -   On the Quotes tab, click **New Quote**.
2.  Enter the quote details, such as the name, account, opportunity, and status.
3.  If needed, select any custom fields that your admin enabled.
4.  Save your changes.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Configure an Insurance Quote

To configure a quote, add products and edit product configurations.

### Required Editions

| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To configure a quote: | 
Create Orders from Quotes

AND

Price and Tax Calculation for Quoting

 |

1.  On the quote record page, click **Browse Catalogs**.
2.  Select a price book, and then save your selection.
3.  Select a product catalog, and then click **Next**.
4.  Click **Add** on the product that you want to add to the quote.
5.  After adding the products, click **Save Quote**.
6.  Configure product details and calculate premiums.
    1.  Click the gear icon next to **Add**.
    2.  Enter the configuration details.
    3.  To view pricing based on the product configuration details that you entered, click **Update Prices**.
    4.  After configuring the product, click **Save & Exit**.
7.  After configuring the product details for all products , click **Save Quote**.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Modify Insurance Quote Line Items

Make changes to the line items on a quote by configuring the line item.

### Required Editions

| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To modify quote line items: | 
Create Orders from Quotes

AND

Price and Tax Calulation for Quoting

 |

Note You can't deep clone a Digital Insurance quote or order its quote line items by the product code.

1.  On the quote record, on the Quote Line Items tab, select **Configure** from the list on the product or line item that you want to modify.
2.  Make the changes.
3.  To view pricing based on the product configuration details that you entered, click **Update Prices**.
4.  Click **Save & Exit**.
