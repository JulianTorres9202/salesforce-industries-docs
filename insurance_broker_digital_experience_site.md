# Broker Digital Experience Site

Your brokers and agents need access to quotes, policies, and accounts they work with to sell insurance products and manage existing policies. Use the Digital Experience template and components to get you started in building a custom Digital Experience site that meets those needs.

**Where:** The Broker Digital Experience Site is built on the Lightning design system; it doesn't work on Newport. If you have a Vlocity Insurance license, you can access the template and components that comprise this feature.

You get the Broker Digital Experience template by installing the Digital Experience unmanaged package. To learn how, see [Install the Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_install_the_insurance_fsc_unmanaged_package_650038.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "To start using templates to create an Experience site for your users, install the Insurance FSC unmanaged package.").

**Who:** Your Vlocity developers configure the Broker site. Brokers and agents you've granted credentials to can log on to the site.

**Why:** The Broker Digital Experience site lets agents and brokers who sell your insurance products access the accounts they're working on, generate quotes, sell policies, and manage existing policies.

We provide a template and a set of components to get you started. But we expect that you'll want to customize the components we provide and possibly create your own.

We left it simple because we expect you to customize it to make it your own.

**How:** Here's an overview of how you use the template and components to build your own Broker site:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

1.  Install the Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package to get access to the Broker Experience template.
    
    [Install the Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_install_the_insurance_fsc_unmanaged_package_650038.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "To start using templates to create an Experience site for your users, install the Insurance FSC unmanaged package.")
    
2.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)
    
    Create a Broker site for your users.
    
    [Create Your Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_create_your_experience_site_650072.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "To start using the templates provided in the unmanaged package, create an Experience site for your users.")
    
3.  Save a template based on the site you created.
    
    [Save Your Experience Site Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_save_your_experience_site_template_650229.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Save a template so that you can customize the Experience site for your users. This step is necessary so that you can uninstall the unmanaged package.")
    
4.  Customize the Digital Experience components to fit your brand and your users' needs. Create your own components if you need them.
    
    [Digital Experience Site Component Customization Overview](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_digital_experience_site_component_customization_overview_649514.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "We've provided out-of-the-box components for Insurance Experience sites, but we know you'll probably want to brand them with your logos, icons, colors, and fonts for your customers to see. You can also customize the data that's displayed.")
    
5.  Using your custom template and custom components, assign the template you saved in step 3 to your Broker site and add your custom components to it.
    
    [Add Customized Components to the Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_add_customized_components_to_the_site_649927.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "When you customize Experience components (or create new ones), you need to add them to your experience site so your end users can see them.")
    
6.  When it's ready, publish your Broker site and start granting your brokers and agents access to it.
