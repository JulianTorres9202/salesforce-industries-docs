# Insurance Property & Casualty Application Suite Customer Interaction

When a customer contacts a service agent they can verify the policyholder details.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)After receiving a call from an individual customer:

1.  Open the Interaction Launcher from the utility bar at the bottom.
2.  In the **POLICYHOLDER** section, enter any or all of the required information about the customer and click **Search**.
3.  The results are displayed and you need to click **Verify** to corroborate customer information.
4.  Based on customer answers, click the checkmark to verify relevant details and then click **Launch Console** to view complete customer information.
