# Modify Details and Attributes on a Grandchild Insured Party or Insured Item

You can modify the attributes and details of a Grandchild insured item or insured party to provide your customer with exactly the coverage they need.

1.  On the insured item, click the name of the grandchild insured party or insured item.
2.  Select **Edit Details**.
3.  Make the required changes.
4.  Click **Save**.
