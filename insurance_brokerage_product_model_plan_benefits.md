# Product Model for Plan Benefits

In Product Catalog Manager, build a product model to manage data entry for plan benefits across health-related coverages

Model plan benefits under Medical, Dental, and Vision coverage for an Employee Benefits health policy by using the [sample plan benefit information](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_sample_benefits_information.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Use the plan benefits information to model the medical, dental, and vision coverage products under the Employee Benefits root product.").

-   **[Prerequisites](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_modelling_prerequisites.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Here are a few things to consider before building a product model.
-   **[Create a Root Product](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_root_product_create.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create a root product to organize multiple coverage and benefits products into a hierarchical structure. You can add child products beneath a bundled product. This helps you model your products in a way that is aligned with your business needs.
-   **[Create Product Component Group](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_pcg_create.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create a product component group to organize product components and make it easier to configure coverages.
-   **[Create Product Structure for Benefits](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_benefits_product__structure_create.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create a coverage product structure by defining product components, coverage specifications, and child components. The structure represents the relationships between components and child components. Use this procedure to create all coverage specifications.
-   **[Add the Coverage Specifications to the Product Component Group](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_coverage_specification_add.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Add the child components, which are coverage specifications, to a product component group so that the associated products are grouped together.
-   **[Medical Coverage Product Structure](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_medical_coverage_structure.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Model the medical coverage product structure under the Employee Benefits root product.
-   **[Plan Benefits Sample Information](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_sample_benefits_information.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use the plan benefits information to model the medical, dental, and vision coverage products under the Employee Benefits root product.
