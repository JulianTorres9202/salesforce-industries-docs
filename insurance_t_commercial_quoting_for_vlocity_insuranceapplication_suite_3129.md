# Commercial Quoting for Insurance Application Suite

All new commercial products and a business process in the Lex Console to create commercial quotes and rate them.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**Where:** This feature is available in the Insurance Application Suite under Property and Casualty Application.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**Why:** Commercial products often have complex product structures incorporating multiple products and multi-level insured item hierarchies with pricing enabled at each level of the hierarchy. Use the Lex Console to run our guided flow that uses out-of-the-box LWCs to add products, rate, and reprice them to create your quotes.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**How:** This feature is auto-enabled in the reference org that [you can request](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs "Insurance business apps are pre-built solutions created with industry experts to help you modernize your business and focus more on your customers. These apps are delivered through reference orgs that include the Insurance managed package. To accelerate your digital transformation, these reference orgs also have sample configuration and seed data you can migrate into your production org. Industries Business App reference orgs are only available to current customers and partners.") for the Property & Casualty business app. We've added new commercial products for Essential Business, Commercial Auto, and General Liability.

Commercial quoting is based on an OmniStudio OmniScript that you can find under:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

-   Type/Subtype: InsCommercial/QuoteCreation
    
-   OmniScript Name: Commercial Quote Creation
