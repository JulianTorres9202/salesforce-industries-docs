# Update the Claim Line Item's Record Type Status

For the Claim Line Item record type, add values to the Status picklist.

1.  From **Setup**, click the **Object Manager** tab.
2.  Click **Claim Line Item**.
3.  Click **Record Types**.
4.  Click **Claim Line Item**.
5.  In the **Picklists Available for Editing** section, click **Edit** next to **Status**.
6.  Move all of the **Available Values** to the **Selected Values** side.
7.  In the dropdown marked **Default**, select **New**.
8.  Click **Save**.
