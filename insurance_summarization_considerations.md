## Einstein Trust Layer Service Support

The Insurance Service Assistance agent template supports the Trust Layer services supported by the [Agentforce Employee Agent](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.agent_employee_agent_considerations.htm&language=en_US&type=5) type.

## Usage Types Billed By Insurance Service Assistance Agents

The Insurance Service Assistance agent uses generative AI and Data Cloud to ingest, store, and process data. You’re billed in these categories when you use Insurance Service Assistance. Before deployment, work with your Salesforce account team to confirm license availability and plan credit usage.

## Feature Limitations

If your org doesn't have multi-currency enabled, Insurance Summarization provides amounts in the currency specified in the Currency Locale field. If your org has multi-currency enabled, Insurance Summarization provides amounts in the currency specified in the Corporate Currency field.
