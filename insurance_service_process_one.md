# Standard Service Process for Insurance

Implement and execute effective service processes for customer satisfaction. To build service processes and configure integrations with external systems is a time-consuming and complex task.

Service agents can launch these service processes and get started with critical customer service operations. Customer community users can directly launch these service processes from a self-service portal and quickly submit their service process requests.

-   **[Request Insurance Proof Service Process](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_request_insurance_proof_service_process.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    ​​With the Request Insurance Proof service process, your customers can request proof of insurance through a straightforward and simplified process.
-   **[Self-Service](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_self_service.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Customer community users initiate service processes directly from a self-service portal, such as the Insurance Client Portal. When you make the service processes available with a self-service portal, your customers can raise service process requests at any time, from anywhere.
