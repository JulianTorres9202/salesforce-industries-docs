# Auto Pay

Policyholders, agents, and admin users can set up automatic payments from the Lex Console.

1.  Open the policy that you want to set up direct billing for and from **Actions**, click **Set-Up Billing**.
2.  Select **Auto Pay** and click **Next**.
3.  Select the bill delivery method and click **Next**.
4.  Now, select your payment method and click **Next**.
5.  Depending on the option you selected, enter the payment details.
6.  Select a payment frequency and click **Next**.
7.  That's it! You see a confirmation that direct billing has been approved. To see your policy, click **View Policy**.
