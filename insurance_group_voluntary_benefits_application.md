# Group Voluntary Benefits Application

Your users can cut down the time they spend on finding the ideal plan and then enrolling employees. We've made intuitive workflows that take out the guesswork with complete guidance for every step. Your service agents can provide better customer service by using the interaction console to quickly get a 360-degree view of the customer.

**Where:** This feature is available in the Insurance Application Suite under Voluntary Benefits Application.

**Who:** This feature is available to Vlocity Insurance administrators and to your customers and service agents.

**Why:** With Group Voluntary Benefits Application, your users can:

-   Create a quote
    
-   Create a contract
    
-   Enroll members
    
-   Support Customers with the Interaction Launcher and Console
    

**How:**[Request an org](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs "Insurance business apps are pre-built solutions created with industry experts to help you modernize your business and focus more on your customers. These apps are delivered through reference orgs that include the Insurance managed package. To accelerate your digital transformation, these reference orgs also have sample configuration and seed data you can migrate into your production org. Industries Business App reference orgs are only available to current customers and partners.") for the Group Voluntary Benefits business app. We've included example workflows for each of these features that you can peruse to create your own. You don't need any configuration to access these workflows.
