# Add Products to a Commercial Quote

Add a product to a commercial quote to start configuring it. You must add a product to a quote before you can add insured items or parties.

1.  From the Quote list page, open the quote you want to add a product to.
2.  Click **Add Product** on the Insurance Commercial Quote LWC.
3.  Select the product (or products) you want to add to the quote, then click **Add**.
