# Get to Know the New Hire Enrollment and Termination Workflow for Group Benefits

The Mid-Term Adjustments Lightning web component gives insurance carrier administrators a straightforward way to:

-   Enroll new hires in the middle of a contract term.
    
-   Terminate policies at any time.
    

-   **[Upload New Hire Census Data for Enrollment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_upload_new_hire_census_data_new_hire_enrollment.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    After a contract is in place, upload group benefits product enrollment data with new hire census members and dependents.
-   **[Terminate a Policy for a Single Member](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_terminate_single_member.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Terminate policies of primary members and associated dependents individually in the middle of a contract. Get notified when the termination is completed and receive a list of errors if termination couldn't be completed.
-   **[Terminate Member Policies in Bulk](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_terminate_members_bulk.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Terminate policies of the primary members and dependents in bulk in the middle of a contract.
