# Define Group Class Eligibility

To provide more granular benefit options to your users when they use group classes, map the Insurance Benefits Eligibility definitions on an Insurance Policy or Insurance Policy Coverage record to the relevant group classes.

To define group class eligibility at the Insurance Policy or Insurance Policy Coverage level, [add a group class to an account](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_benefit_eligibility_group_class_account.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Group classes define a group of employees. To associate a group class with a specific record, add the group class to the account related list.").

1.  From an Insurance Policy or Insurance Policy Coverage record, on the Insurance Policy Benefit Eligibility related list, click **Define Group Class Eligibility**.
2.  To create mappings between group classes and eligibility definitions, use the Group Class Name and Eligibility Definition dropdown menus.
    
    The available group classes and eligibility definitions are pulled from the account record.
    
3.  To add additional mappings, click **Add**.
    
    You can associate a group class to only one eligibility definition.
    
4.  Save your work.
