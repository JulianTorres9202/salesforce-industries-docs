# 2024 Insurance Release Notes

See these release notes for the Insurance releases in 2024.

-   **[Insurance Summer ‘24 Release Notes](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_summer_24_release_notes.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Discover how you can provide your customers with quick access to policy and claim details with the help of the Financial Services Client Portal for FSC Insurance. Use Einstein Copilot for Insurance to help your customer service representatives save time and effort by generating quick record summaries. Gain insights and view customer interactions with marketing emails by using the Engagement Timeline.
-   **[Insurance Spring '24 Release Notes](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_spring_24_release_notes.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use flows to verify callers and producers, rate large group quotes, and enroll census members in plans. Delete census members in bulk, enable customers to self-enroll, and identify the correct pricing procedure to use during the policy lifecycle with the help of new and enhanced services. Help agents navigate claim records with the Insurance Agent Console.
-   **[Insurance Winter '24 Release Notes](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_winter_24_release_notes.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Simplify large group quoting and enrollment workflows with new and enhanced Lightning web components. Leverage the flexibility to enroll new hires or terminate policies throughout the contract term. Calculate and view employee and employer contributions during bulk and new hire enrollment. Quickly create relationship graphs for Insurance customers with the help of templates.
