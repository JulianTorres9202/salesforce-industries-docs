# Manage Workers' Compensation Claims with New and Changed Objects

Track claimant earnings that are used to calculate compensation, establish recurring payment schedules, and track formal progress toward someone's return to work with party certified capacity.

**Where:** This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package.

**Who:** Users need the Claims Management add-on license to use this feature.

**Why:** Workers' compensation claims involve extra layers of administration and record-keeping. New and updated standard objects make compliance easier for claims adjusters and help get timely, accurate payments to injured workers.
