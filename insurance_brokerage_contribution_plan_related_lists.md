# Add Insurance Contribution Plan Related Lists to Policy Pages

To make the Insurance Contribution Plans related lists available to your users, add them to the page layouts.

### Required Editions

| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To use contribution plan: | Insurance Policy Management User |

1.  From the object management settings for Insurance Policy object, go to **Page Layouts**.
2.  On the layout assigned to your users, click **Edit**.
3.  Click **Related Lists**.
4.  Drag **Insurance Contribution Plans** from the available options and drop it onto the layout.
5.  Save your changes.
6.  Similarly, add the Insurance Policy Contribution Plan related list for the Insurance Policy Coverage object.
