# Workers Compensation Product for Commercial Insurance

The Commercial line of business now comes with a fully functional example of the Workers Compensation product models, rating procedures, and business processes for quoting.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**Where:** This feature is available in the Insurance Application Suite under Property and Casualty Application.

**Who:** This feature is available to admin users and agents.

**Why:** Brokers and admin users can now save time and effort by quickly creating a quote for workers compensation product in Commercial insurance.

**How:** The product model and the rating procedure that calculate prices for Workers Compensation products at runtime underpin all the business processes in this line of business:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

-   Product Model: Workers Compensation
    
-   Rating Procedure: WorkersCompensationNCCIRating
