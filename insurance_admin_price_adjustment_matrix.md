# Modify the Price Adjustment Matrix

To account for different products, different territories, and other factors, modify your price adjustment matrix. Use this information to set discounts and other price adjustments.

| To add and use price adjustment matrices: | Salesforce Pricing Design Time |
| To create pricing recipes: | Salesforce Pricing Design Time |

1.  From Setup, in the Quick Find box, enter Pricing, and then select **Pricing Recipes**.
2.  Verify that NGPDefaultRecipe is set as the default recipe.
3.  Click **NGPDefaultRecipe** to open it, and then click the **Price Adjustment Matrix** tab.
4.  Click **Modify**, and then add the required decision matrices.
5.  Save your work.
