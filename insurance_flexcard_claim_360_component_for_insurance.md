# FlexCard-Based Claim 360 Component for Insurance

To configure the FlexCard-based Claim 360 component, assign OmniStudio permissions and the FSC Insurance permission set license to users, and customize the FlexCards, Integration Procedures, and Omnistudio Data Mappers. FlexCards provide an improved user experience and simpler configurations. You can easily embed FlexCards in other FlexCards and use flyouts to show more details on demand.

-   **[Customize the FlexCard-Based Claim 360 Component](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_customize_the_flexcardbased_claim_360_component.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Customize the OmniStudio FlexCards, Integration Procedures, and Omnistudio Data Mappers to suit your business needs.
-   **[Configure FlexCard-Based Claim 360 in Lightning Experience](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_configure_flexcardbased_claim_360_in_lightning_experience.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    In Lightning App Builder, drag FlexCard from the Components panel to the Lightning page canvas location where you want to position the component.
