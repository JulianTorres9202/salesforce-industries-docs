# Manage Complex Large Group Quotes with the Large Group Quote Lightning Web Component

Quickly configure group classes, and build, price, and view complex quotes with the large group quoting LWC.

**Where:** This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package and the Insurance Industries Extension managed package.

**Why:** Large group quotes can have complex product structures. The Large Group Quote LWC provides your users with a concise view of large group quotes. Users can expand and minimize quote line items at every coverage level and view or modify details about coverages in the coverage detail side panel. Your users can also save time by configuring group classes for one or more plans right from the LWC.
