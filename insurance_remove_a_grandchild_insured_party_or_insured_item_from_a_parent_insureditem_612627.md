# Remove a Grandchild Insured Party or Insured Item from a Parent Insured Item

You can remove a grandchild insured item or insured party from a parent insured item to provide your customer with exactly the coverage they need.

1.  On the insured item, click the name of the grandchild insured party or insured item.
2.  Select **Remove from** InsuredItemName.
    
    The grandchild insured party or insured item disappears from the parent insured item.
