# Claim Record Page

Your policyholders have a claim record page for each of their claims.

On this page, your policyholders can:

-   See an overview of the claim.
    
-   View the status of the claim.
    
-   View claim item details.
    
-   View agent or producer details.
    
-   Send a message to the agent or producer.
    
-   Upload relevant documents for the claim.
    

Note The self-service portal has only a single-currency support. You must configure multi-currency support, if required.
