# Generate Proof of Insurance

Use the Agentforce for Financial Services: Insurance Service Assistance to generate a proof of insurance document and attach it to a policy.

After they're generated, proof of insurance documents are available in the **Notes & Attachments** section of a policy record.

1.  From any page, click the Agentforce icon to launch the agent.
2.  Ask the agent questions such as these:
    
    Generate proof of insurance for account John Doe.
    
    Please create proof of insurance for account John Doe.
    
3.  Select a policy from the list presented by the agent and click **Submit**.
4.  Review the list of assets associated with the policy and click **Submit**.
5.  Review the list of policy participants and click **Submit**.
6.  When the agent finishes creating the proof of insurance, select the link that's displayed to view the document on the corresponding policy record.
    
    Select the **Details** tab then **Notes & Attachments**.
