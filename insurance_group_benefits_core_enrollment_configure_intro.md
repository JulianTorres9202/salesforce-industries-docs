# Configure Group Benefits Enrollment

Create a workflow that gives insurance carrier administrators a straightforward way to enroll members of the group along with their dependents into selected plans and coverages.

The Enrollment Lightning web component works with the Insurance Group Enrollment API to support bulk enrollment asynchronously. You can enroll up to 10,000 members across 30 distinct plans and 10 product categories. You’ll be notified when the process is ‌complete. In case of failure, a CSV file with detailed information is provided.
