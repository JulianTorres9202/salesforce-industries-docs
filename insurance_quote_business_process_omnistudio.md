# Quote Business Process

We've created a bunch of business processes for you to examine, use as examples, and extend to create your own business processes for auto insurance in Vlocity.

All our screenshots are currently in Lightning style. Your spin may look different because it's using the Newport style in the Broker Portal and Customer Portal.

In Vlocity Auto, you can create new quotes from:
