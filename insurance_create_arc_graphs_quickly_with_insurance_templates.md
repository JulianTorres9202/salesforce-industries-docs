# Create Actionable Graphs for Insurance Customers Quickly with Templates

Save time when creating relationship graphs in the Actionable Relationship Center by using templates. Two new templates give you a head start on building graphs by providing you with pre-configured nodes that show relationships for your Insurance Personal Lines and Insurance Commercial Lines clients.

**Where:** This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions.

**Who:** Users need the Financial Services Cloud Standard permission set to use this feature.

**How:** In Setup, create a new Actionable Relationship Center relationship graph. Select **Default Templates** and then select **Insurance Personal Lines**.

Or select **Insurance Commercial Lines**.
