# Member Enrollment Through the Policyholder Site

If you are an employee of a group associated with a Census in an Active Contract that has the User Community Activated, you have access to a nifty policyholder digital Experience Site. You can log into the site and perform multiple tasks, including enrolling yourself into a policy of your choice.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)When the contract for your enrollment is created, you see the option to enroll yourself and any dependents into various plans.

1.  To begin, navigate to the policyholder Home page and click **Start Enrollment**.
2.  Next, review your information and check the details of your dependents. You can't make any changes to your information here but you can change dependent details.
    
    Select **I confirm that the above mentioned details are accurate and agree for legal use on policy** and click **Next**.
    
3.  Select who you'd like to include in medical plans. For this example, we're going to select the employee and family. Select the family members.
    
    Now select a medical plan to enroll in and click **Add to cart**. If you don't If you don't want to enroll in any medical plans, select **Opt out of Medical Plans** and then click **Add to cart**.
    
4.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Select who you'd like to include in dental plans. For this example, we're going to select the employee only.
    
    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Now select a dental plan to enroll in and click **Add to cart**. If you don't If you don't want to enroll in any dental plans, select **Opt out of Dental Plans** and then click **Add to cart**.
    
5.  Select a member option for the vision plan. For this example, we're going to select the employee and spouse.
    
    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Now select a vision plan to enroll in and click **Add to cart**. If you don't If you don't want to enroll in any vision plans, select **Opt out of Vision Plans** and then click **Add to cart**.
    
6.  Now select members for life plans. For this example, we're going to select the employee only.
    
    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Now select a life plan to enroll in and click **Add to cart**. If you don't If you don't want to enroll in any life plans, select **Opt out of Life Plans** and then click **Add to cart**.
    
7.  Now select any additional plans. These are optional plans available only for the primary member. When you're done, click **Add to cart**.
8.  Review the plans you've chosen and select **I have reviewed the policies and accept these as my final selection**. Click **Next**.
9.  You're done! You see a summary of your plans and monthly premium. To go back to the Home page and see your policies, click **Next**.
