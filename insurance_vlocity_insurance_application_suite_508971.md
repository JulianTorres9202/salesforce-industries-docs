# See Insurance in Action with the Insurance Application Suite

Want to get a jump on configuring Insurance to rate, quote, and sell insurance products, and administer sold policies? The Application Suite offers examples of fully built applications that can guide you as you plan your own implementations.

Important Insurance & Financial Services processes and digital assets are no longer available to download. To accelerate your digital transformation, [request an org](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs "Insurance business apps are pre-built solutions created with industry experts to help you modernize your business and focus more on your customers. These apps are delivered through reference orgs that include the Insurance managed package. To accelerate your digital transformation, these reference orgs also have sample configuration and seed data you can migrate into your production org. Industries Business App reference orgs are only available to current customers and partners.") with the Property & Casualty, Property & Casualty with Core OmniStudio, or Group Voluntary Benefits business app.

Each application contains the core Insurance configurations that form the basis of all implementations. Each application contains these and other configurations specific to its type of insurance.

-   Attributes
    
-   Product models
    
-   Rating procedures
    
-   Quoting business processes
    
-   Policy administration business processes
    
-   Claims business processes

## Available Applications

Here are the applications you can examine and explore:

-   **[Insurance Property & Casualty Application Overview](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_propertycasualty_application_overview_509004.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Insurance Property & Casualty Application provides examples of insurance applications for a number of lines of business that property and casualty insurers handle. It also includes the Commercial line of business.
-   **[Insurance Property & Casualty Application with OmniStudio Overview](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_property_casualty_application_with_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Insurance Property & Casualty Application provides examples of insurance applications for a number of lines of business that property and casualty insurers handle.
-   **[Group Voluntary Benefits Application with OmniStudio Overview](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_group_voluntary_benefits_application_overview_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    We know it's far from simple to manage group plans for your employees. That's why we've created Group Voluntary Benefits Application to simplify how you manage plans and enroll employees.
-   **[Commercial Application Overview](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_commercial_application_overview_526039.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Commercial Application provides a fully functional example of Commercial Auto, Essential Business and General Liability product models, rating procedures, and business processes for quoting, and issuing, policies.
