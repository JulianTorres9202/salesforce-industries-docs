# Policyholder Digital Experience Site Home Page

When a policyholder logs into their Policyholder site, they land on the Home page.

Out of the box, the Home page looks like this:

On this page, your users can:

-   See recent activity on their account
    
-   See a list of their policies
    
    If policies exist, the policy name is a link to the policy record page
    
-   View opens claims for their account
    
    If a claim exists, the claim name is a link to the claim record page
    
-   See contact information for their agent
    
-   Click links to helpful articles
    
-   Update account information
    
-   Click links to OmniScripts or websites
    

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Note

Remember, you must customize these components for your own Policyholder site.
