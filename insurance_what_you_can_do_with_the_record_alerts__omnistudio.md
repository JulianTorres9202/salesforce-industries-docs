# What You Can Do With the Record Alerts?

Property & Casualty application is customised to support these record alerts on Insurance Policy 360 console:

|   | 
Alert Type

 |
| --- | --- |
| 

Account Level

 | 

Policy expiration date is less than 30 days away for the policies that the account holds.

 |
| 

Count of open claims for the account.

 |   |
| 

Policy premium payment date is less than 30 days away.

 |   |
| 

Policy Level

 | 

Open claims for the policy.

 |
| 

Policy expiry is less than 30 days away for the policy.

 |   |
| 

Policy premium payment is due in 30 days.

 |   |
