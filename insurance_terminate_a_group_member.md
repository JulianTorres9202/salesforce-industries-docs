# Terminate A Group Member

Cancel the policy and update the term premium for a group member and their dependents.

1.  From the Mid-Term Adjustments tab on the Group Account page, click **Terminate Member**.
2.  In the Terminate Member search bar, find the member.
3.  Enter a termination date within the policy’s effective date range. Click **Terminate** and then confirm the termination.
    
    A bell notification lets you know that the termination processing completed.
    
4.  After the policy termination, view the policy details to confirm the policy’s canceled status and updated term premium.
