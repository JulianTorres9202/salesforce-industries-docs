# Save Your Experience Site Template

Save a template so that you can customize the Experience site for your users. This step is necessary so that you can uninstall the unmanaged package.

The highlighted area in this flow shows where you are in the process of setting up your Experience site:

1.  On the Experience Builder page, open the **Settings** (gear) menu.
2.  Select **Developer** on the left side of the window.
3.  On the **Export a Template** tab, enter the required information.
4.  Click **Export**.
