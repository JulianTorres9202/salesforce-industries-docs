# Create a Quote from the Broker Experience Site

Brokers and agents can quickly create a quote directly from the Broker Experience site using a guided flow.

1.  Log into the broker Experience site and under **Quick Actions**, click **Create a Quote**.
2.  Search for a group account or you can also create a new one.
    
    When you're done, click **Next**.
    
3.  Enter quote details and click **Next**.
4.  Select plans and click **Next**.
5.  It's as simple as that. You see the quote confirmation. To see quote details, click **View Quote**.
    
    After the broker creates the quote, the admin user (Captive Agent) can add the census and price the quote for the broker.
