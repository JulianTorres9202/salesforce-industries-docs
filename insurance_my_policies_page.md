# My Policies Page

Your policyholders have a list view of their policies.

On this page, your policyholders can:

-   See a summary of all the policies.
    
-   Refine the list view by using a search keyword such as the policy name to filter the policies.
    
-   View a policy record in detail by using the policy name.
