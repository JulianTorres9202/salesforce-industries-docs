# Property Quote Business Process

We've created a quote business process for Homeowners and Renters insurance for you to examine, use as examples, and extend to create your own business processes for property insurance in Vlocity.

Our screenshots are currently in Lightning style. Your spin may look different because it's using the Newport style in the Broker Portal and Customer Portal.

In Property, you can create new quotes from:
