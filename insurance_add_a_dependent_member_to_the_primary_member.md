# Add a Dependent Member to the Primary Member

You can add a dependent member to an existing primary member in the census.

1.  Click the action menu for the primary member and select **Add Dependent**.
2.  In the **Personal Information** tab, enter the personal information of the dependent member.
3.  In the **Other Information** tab, from the **Relationship to Primary Member** picklist, choose an appropriate value. If the primary member has opted out of all plans, you’ll see a message stating that the dependent member has also opted out of all plans.
4.  To opt a dependent out of a plan that the primary member has opted for, turn off **Opt in**.
    
    Note You can’t choose a plan that the primary member hasn’t opted for, but you can select a different set of optional coverage selections for a dependent.
    
5.  Click **Next**.
6.  In the confirmation window, click **Add Dependent**.
