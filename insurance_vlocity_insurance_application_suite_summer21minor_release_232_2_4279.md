# Vlocity Insurance Application Suite Summer '21 Minor Release 232.2

This section describes key fixes and enhancements for the Vlocity Insurance Application Suite in this minor release.

-   **[Property and Casualty Application](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_property_and_casualty_application_4912.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Send policy renewal reminders, see producer commissions while generating quotes, and more in the Property and Casualty Application.
-   **[Voluntary Benefits Application](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_voluntary_benefits_application_5003.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Offer add-on products to your policyholders when they enroll themselves in insurance plans, and empower your service agents with the new harmonized interaction console.
