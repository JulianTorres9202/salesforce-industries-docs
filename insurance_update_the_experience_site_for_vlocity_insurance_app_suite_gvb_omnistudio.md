# Update the Experience Site for Vlocity Insurance Application Suite Group Voluntary Benefits

After you receive your spin, you need to complete the following steps twice: once for the Digital Broker Self-Service Portal and once for the Digital Policyholder Self-Service Portal.

1.  Go to Setup.
2.  In **Quick Find** , search All Communities.
3.  Click **Builder** for one of the communities. The Experience Builder launches.
4.  In the Experience Builder, click Settings, select the **I understand the changes applied in this update** checkbox and then click **Update**.
5.  On the modal that appears, click **Update**.
6.  Once the update is done, publish your community. Click **Publish** in the top right corner of the Experience Builder.
