# Policy Record Page

Your policyholders have a policy record page for each of their policies.

On this page, your policyholders can:

-   See the overview of the policy.
    
-   View the status of the policy.
    
-   View root-level coverages of the policy.
    
-   View policy asset details.
    
-   Use quick links to perform tasks in the context of the page. For example, they can add a claim, modify coverages, or cancel a policy in the record page.
    
-   View agent or producer details.
    
-   Send a message to the agent or producer.
    

Note The self-service portal has only single-currency support. You must configure multi-currency support, if required.
