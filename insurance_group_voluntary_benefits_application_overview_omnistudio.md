# Group Voluntary Benefits Application with OmniStudio Overview

We know it's far from simple to manage group plans for your employees. That's why we've created Group Voluntary Benefits Application to simplify how you manage plans and enroll employees.

With all the numerous coverages and attributes in group plans, you probably want to try multiple combinations before you create a plan that works best for your organization and employees. To help you, we've created some example workflows in Group Voluntary Benefits Application that help you arrive at your ideal plan that much faster.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Business Processes

We have defined a reference group account called INS Group along with Quote and Enrollment Census to understand the different business processes on how it works.

In this release of Group Voluntary Benefits Application, you can:

-   Create a quote
    
    Quickly and efficiently configure, reprice, and view a quote for group plans.
    
-   Create a contract
    
    Your contract in PDF or MS Word format is just one click away. Yes, we've made it that easy for you.
    
-   Enroll members
    
    Upload group benefits product enrollment data with census members and dependents by using the Enrollment Census Lightning web component.
    
-   Enroll a new hire
    
    Enroll new hires in the middle of a contract term.
    
-   Terminate a group member
    
    Cancel the policy and update the term premium for a group member and their dependents.
