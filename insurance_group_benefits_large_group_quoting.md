# Group Benefits Large Group Quoting

Quickly and efficiently configure, reprice, and view large group quotes to help your customers provide their employees with exactly the coverage they need.

Large group quoting is supported for member-based and summary-based quotes.

-   **[Large Group Quote Lightning Web Component](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_large_group_quote_lightning_web_component.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Large Group Quote LWC is specially designed to help you build complex quotes. It features a side panel for editing line items. Buttons for common actions display prominently so your users can issue policies, create quote versions, compare quote version histories, and add additional plans with ease.
-   **[Large Group Census Rating Flows](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_large_group_census_rating_flows.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Support asynchronous member-based and summary-based rating with census rating flows.
