# Create Product Component Group

Create a product component group to organize product components and make it easier to configure coverages.

1.  On the Details tab of the product record page, click New on the Product Component Group related list.
2.  Enter the Product Component Group information for Coverages and save your changes.
    
    When entering product component group details, specify the sequence to define the order of the product component groups on the Product Structure page. For example, specify 1 to show Coverages as the first item under Employee Benefits.
