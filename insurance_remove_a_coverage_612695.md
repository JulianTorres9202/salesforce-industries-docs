# Remove a Coverage

You can remove a coverage if it is no longer needed.

1.  Click **Show Coverages** on the product or insured item you want to remove coverage from.
    
    Any optional coverages that can be removed have a **Remove** button at the right.
    
2.  Click the **Remove** button next to the coverage you're removing.
