# Claims Management Editions and Permission Sets

Give users access to Claims Management features. Review the supported edition and permission sets for Claims Management.

## User Interface

Claims Management is available in Lightning Experience.

## Supported Editions

Claims Management is available in Enterprise, Unlimited, and Developer Editions of Digital Insurance Platform with the DigitalInsuranceClaimsManagementAddon, DigitalInsuranceClaimManagementPCAddOn, and DigitalInsuranceClaimManagementCCAddOn.

## Claims Management Permission Sets
