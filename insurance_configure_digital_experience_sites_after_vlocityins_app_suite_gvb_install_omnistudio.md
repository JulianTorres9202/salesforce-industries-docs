# Configure Your Digital Experience Sites After Vlocity Insurance Application Suite Group Voluntary Benefits Installation

There are a few configuration steps for the Digital Experience Sites that you create for your users.

-   [Enable Standard External Profiles for Group Voluntary Benefits](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_enable_standard_external_profiles_for_group_voluntary_benefits_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Before you can start letting users access your new Experience Site, you need to enable the Digital Experience setting.")
    
    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Before you can start letting users access your new Experience Site, you need to enable the Digital Experience setting.

    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)After you receive your spin, you need to complete the following steps twice: once for the Digital Broker Self-Service Portal and once for the Digital Policyholder Self-Service Portal.
    

-   **[Enable Standard External Profiles for Group Voluntary Benefits](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_enable_standard_external_profiles_for_group_voluntary_benefits_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Before you can start letting users access your new Experience Site, you need to enable the Digital Experience setting.
