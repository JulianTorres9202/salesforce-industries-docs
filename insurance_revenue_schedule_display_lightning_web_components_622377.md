# Revenue Schedule Display Lightning Web Components

You can view the **Revenue Schedule** for a policy on the Policy (Asset) record. The **Revenue Schedule** tab displays two Lightning Web Components (LWC): Vlocity Insurance Revenue Charts and Vlocity Insurance Revenue Schedule.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Vlocity Insurance Revenue Charts

The Vlocity Insurance Revenue Charts LWC has two tabs: **Per Period** and **Total Revenue**.

The **Per Period** tab shows you a bar graph representation of the dollar amount of revenue earned in each period of the policy:

The **Total Revenue** tab shows you a line graph of the Total Earned Revenue and Total Unearned Revenue over the lifespan of the policy:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Vlocity Insurance Revenue Schedule

You can see the Vlocity Insurance Revenue Schedule LWC directly below the charts. This component displays information in four columns:

-   **Date**
    
-   **Earned in Period**
    
-   **Total Earned Revenue**
    
-   **Total Unearned Revenue**
