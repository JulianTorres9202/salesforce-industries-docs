# Group Benefits Broker Digital Experience Site

Your network of agents and brokers who sell your insurance products need access to specific features and functions to service their accounts. The Broker Experience site on the Experience Cloud can give them that access.

Tip To use the Broker Experience template, you must install the Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package.

You can brand the components with your colors, fonts, logo, and other styles. You can also customize the substance of the components by adding or removing data, changing the data source, adding new elements, and more. To learn how, see Digital Experience Site Component Customization Overview.

After you've customized the components, you can set up your site. To learn how, see Create Your Experience Site.

Important

We recommend that you use the Experience Builder to create and save your own custom template for your Broker Experience.

The rest of this topic describes the out-of-the-box Broker Experience site.

Note Digital Experience templates don't include Winter ’24 features. To use Winter ’24 features, assign Broker or HR Portal users [permission sets](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_insurance_permission_sets_590543.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Permission set licenses extend the functionality of user licenses. With permission set licenses, you can assign more permissions to users than what their user license supports.") and configure your portal to provide access new features. Large Group Enrollment isn't supported on Broker or HR Portal sites.

## What Agents and Brokers Can Do in the Broker Site

We've created an out-of-the-box Broker Experience site for you to refer, modify and even extend to make it your own. When a broker logs in to the site, they see the Home Page.

From this page, brokers can:

-   See a snapshot of their book of business
-   See a list of recent renewals
-   See an overview of their accounts
-   [Create a quote](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_quote_business_process_for_gbv.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Quickly and efficiently configure, reprice, and view a quote for group plans.")
-   See the total enrollment by plan

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Manage Accounts

A broker can click the Account tab to see and manage their accounts. The Accounts page looks like this:

Broker can do the following things on this page:

-   Sort and filter the list of accounts
    
-   Search for a specific account or accounts
    
-   Click an account to see details
    
    The Account detail page looks like this:
    
    Brokers can do the following things on this page:
    
    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)
    -   View quotes attached to this account
        
    -   View policies attached to this account
        
    -   View account details
        
    -   View related records
        
    -   Do things available on the Actions panel
        
    -   See recent activity for the account on the Account panel

## Manage Existing Policies

A broker can click the Policies tab to view, update, and manage existing policies. The Policies page looks like this:

Brokers can do the following things on this page:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

-   Sort and filter the list of policy
    
-   Search for a specific policy or policies
    
-   Click a policy to see details
    
    The Policy detail page looks like this:
    
    Brokers can do things on this page:
    
    -   View the root insured item and its coverages
        
    -   View insured items and their coverages
        
    -   Modify the policy
        
    -   View the rules log
        
    -   Move the quote from one state to the next on the colored component above the tabs
