# Activate Record Pages for the Unmanaged Package

Many of the pre-configured record pages are installed automatically in the unmanaged package, however, you still need to clone and activate them.

1.  From **Setup**, in the **Quick Find** box, enter Lightning App Builder.
2.  Click **Lightning App Builder**.
3.  Click **Clone** next to the record page name.
4.  Change the **Label** and **Developer Name** or use default values set by the system.
5.  Click **Save**.
6.  The **Page Saved** dialog box opens. Click **Activate**.
7.  On the **Activation** page, click **App, Record Type, and Profile**, then click **Assign to Apps, Record Types, and Profiles**.
8.  On the **Select Apps** window, choose the apps you want to have access to this record, then click **Next**.
9.  On the **Select Form Factor** window, choose the form factor that should have access to the record page, then click **Next**.
10.  On the **Selected Record Types** window, select all the record types you want to appear on this record's page, then click **Next**.
11.  On the **Selected Profiles** window, select all the user profiles you want to have access to this record, then click **Next**.
12.  On the **Review Assignments** window, confirm that you've made all the correct selections for this record based on your business needs, then click **Save**.
13.  Repeat these steps to clone and activate all of the record pages for your org.
