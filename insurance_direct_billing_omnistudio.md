# Direct Billing

Policyholders, agents, and admin users can set up direct billing from the Lex Console.

1.  Open the policy that you want to set up direct billing for and from **Actions**, click **Set-Up Billing**.
2.  Select **Direct Billing** and click **Next**.
3.  Select the bill delivery method and click **Next**.
4.  Now, select your payment frequency and click **Next**.
5.  That's it! You see a confirmation that direct billing has been approved. To see your policy, click **View Policy**.
