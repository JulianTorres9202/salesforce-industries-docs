# Claims Dashboards

Use dashboards to focus on open tasks and claims that need your attention so you can close them quickly.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Supervisor Dashboard

Use out-of-the-box functionality for claims handlers and adjusters to see open claims that need urgent attention and can be pushed to a quick settlement.

1.  See all open tasks for your claim handlers by due date, priority, and type. See task details and click each individual task to navigate to the task record.
    
2.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)
    
    See all open claims for your claim handlers by open duration, status, and loss type. See claim details and click each individual claim to navigate to the claim record.
    
3.  Use filters to narrow down the information you see.
    

Also launch different applications from this dashboard like the agent console, scheduler setup, and analytics studio.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)
