# Activate Vlocity Support Processes

Before you upgrade Insurance, activate Vlocity Support Processes.

1.  From Setup, enter Support Processes in the Quick Find box, and then select **Support Processes**.
2.  Next to Vlocity Support Processes, verify that **Active** is selected.
3.  If Vlocity Support Processes are not active:
    1.  Click **Edit** next to **Vlocity Support Processes**.
    2.  Select **Active**.
    3.  Save the Support Process.
