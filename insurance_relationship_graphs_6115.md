# Relationship Graphs

Refresh Relationship Graphs programmatically, use additional filter operators, and include one-to-many relationships in Timeline graphs.

-   **[JavaScript Method for Refreshing Relationship Graphs](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_javascript_method_for_refreshing_relationship_graphs_6118.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    If you embed a Relationship Graph in another Lightning Web Component, you can use the JavaScript refreshGraph method to refresh the Relationship Graph.
-   **[Relationship Graph Node Filter Operators](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_relationship_graph_node_filter_operators_6139.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    In previous releases, node filters had only an **equals** operator. Beginning with this release, supported operators are **equals**, **not equals**, **like**, **greater than**, **greater than or equal**, **less than**, and **less than or equal**.
-   **[Timeline Graphs Support One-to-Many Relationships](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_timeline_graphs_support_one_to_many_relationships_6172.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    In previous releases, Timeline graphs supported only many-to-many relationships. Beginning with this release, Timeline graphs also support one-to-many relationships.
