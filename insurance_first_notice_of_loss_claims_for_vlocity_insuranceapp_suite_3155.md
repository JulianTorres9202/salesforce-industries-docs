# First Notice of Loss Claims for Vlocity Insurance Application Suite

File a claim for Homeowners and Renters products using a guided flow that is harmonized with the Financial Services Cloud data model.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**Where:** This feature is available in the Insurance Application Suite under Property and Casualty Application.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**Why:** Property loss is a stressful situation and our guided flow wants to alleviate the pain by making the claims process smooth. This guided flow lets you create a claim with a just a few clicks from an enhanced user interface.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)**How:** This feature is auto-enabled in the Property & Casualty Application Suite org that [you can request](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_get_started_app_suite_orgs "Insurance business apps are pre-built solutions created with industry experts to help you modernize your business and focus more on your customers. These apps are delivered through reference orgs that include the Insurance managed package. To accelerate your digital transformation, these reference orgs also have sample configuration and seed data you can migrate into your production org. Industries Business App reference orgs are only available to current customers and partners.").

Property claims are run by an OmniStudio OmniScript that you can find under:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

-   Type/SubType: Insproperty/claimsFSC
    
-   Version name: propertyClaimFNOLFsc
