# Change Attributes on an Existing Insured Item or Insured Party

You can change some insured item attributes based on what's available in the product model.

1.  Click the **Edit** button to the right of the insured item or insured party premium.
2.  On the insured item or Insured party window, make changes to the details and attributes you need.
3.  Click **Save**.
    
    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Note
    
    You can also view and edit Insured Items or Insured Parties on the Insured Items LWC tab.
