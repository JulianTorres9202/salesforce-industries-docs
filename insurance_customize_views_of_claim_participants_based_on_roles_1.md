# Customize Views of Claim Participants Based on Roles

Help claims adjusters focus on what's important for each person or party involved in a claim. Show unique fields and actions for drivers, passengers, repair shops, and other participant roles.

**Where:** This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package.

**Who:** Users need the Claims Management add-on license to use this feature.
