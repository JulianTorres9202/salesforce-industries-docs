# Add the Claim Recoveries Related List to the Claim Page

Claim team members manage claim recovery efforts by using the Claim Recoveries related list.

1.  From Setup, click **Object Manager**.
2.  Click **Claim**.
3.  Click **Page Layouts**, then click **Claim Layout**.
4.  Drag the **Claim Recoveries** related list to the layout.
