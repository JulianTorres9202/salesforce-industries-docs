# Insurance Service Assistance

Provide insurance reps with summaries of policyholder portfolios, overviews of individual insurance quotes, and proof of insurance documents by using the Agentforce for Financial Services: Insurance Service Assistance AI agent. The assistance relies on the Salesforce Insurance data model. Portfolio summaries include all associated policies, assets, and coverages. Quote overviews include line items and related relationships.

Note Insurance Service Assistance agents are available to Salesforce users with the DigitalInsuranceProductAdminAddOn / DigitalInsurancePolicyAdminAddOn / InsuranceProductAdministrationAddOn / InsurancePolicyAdministrationAddOn, UniversalCreditMetering, AgentforceEmployeeAgentAddOn, and EinsteinForFinancialServicesAddOn.

Insurance Service Assistance agents enhance a rep’s efficiency and decision-making while ensuring the confidentiality of sensitive information.

The agent template includes two topics, Insurance Summarization and Policy Servicing, and six actions, [Get Insurance Quote Details](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.agent_ref_fsc_get_quote.htm&language=en_US&type=5), [Summarize Insurance Policyholder](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.agent_ref_fsc_summarize_insurance_policyholder.htm&language=en_US&type=5), [Fetch Asset](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.agent_ref_fsc_fetch_asset.htm&language=en_US&type=5), [Fetch Participant](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.agent_ref_fsc_insurance_fetch_participant.htm&language=en_US&type=5), [Fetch Policies](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.agent_ref_fsc_fetch_policies.htm&language=en_US&type=5) and [Generate Proof of Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.agent_ref_fsc_insurance_generate_proof_of_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5).

Important This feature uses [Einstein Generative AI](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.generative_ai.htm&language=en_US&type=5) and may consume Einstein Requests or Flex Credits or Customer Data Cloud Requests. [Einstein Generative AI Features](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ai.generative_ai_solutions.htm&language=en_US&type=5) and Data Cloud are provided on a separate infrastructure with different security protections and physical hosting locations than the Services. See [Salesforce Trust and Compliance Documentation](https://www.salesforce.com/company/legal/trust-and-compliance-documentation/).

-   **[Set Up Agentforce for Financial Services: Insurance Service Assistance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_agent_setup.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Turn on Einstein and Agentforce, assign permissions, and then activate the agent.
-   **[Create an Agent from the Agentforce Insurance Service Assistance Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_agent_create_from_template.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use the Insurance Service Assistance template of type Agentforce Employee Agent to get an agent up and running for your insurance reps. You can customize the template to include topics and actions specific to your business needs.
-   **[Considerations for Agentforce for Financial Services: Insurance Service Assistance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_summarization_considerations.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    To use the Insurance Service Assistance agent template, consider supported functionality, usage, limitations and allowances, limits, and other issues.
-   **[Summarize Insurance Policyholders and Get Quotes](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_agent_work_with.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use the Agentforce for Financial Services: Insurance Service Assistance agent to summarize policyholder portfolios and get individual insurance quotes.
-   **[Generate Proof of Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_generate_proof_of_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use the Agentforce for Financial Services: Insurance Service Assistance to generate a proof of insurance document and attach it to a policy.
