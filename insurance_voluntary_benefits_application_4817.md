# Voluntary Benefits Application

Give your policyholders a brand new self-service site, enroll members in a new way, and much more in the Voluntary Benefits Application.

Note

Deactivated Interaction Launcher and Console

The interaction launcher and console is not compatible with the new harmonized model that we're on. While we're working on rewiring this component, it won't be available. However, it works just fine in earlier releases.

-   **[Contract Business Component Update](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_contract_business_component_update_4836.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    You can now set the time period for which the contract is valid in the Contract Creation OmniScript.
-   **[Member Enrollment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_member_enrollment_4857.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Empower your policyholders to make their own choices and enroll themselves in plans.
-   **[Policyholder Digital Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policyholder_digital_experience_site_4880.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Give your policyholders access to view and change important information about their account, policies, claims, and billing options with the Policyholder Digital Experience Site.
