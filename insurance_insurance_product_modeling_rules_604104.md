# Insurance Product Modeling Rules

Rules define how an insurance or health product behaves.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)You can design simple or complex rules for your insurance and health products. To learn about the types of rules available and how to apply them to your products, see [Insurance Rules](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_insurance_and_health_rules_606729.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Rules define the way Insurance products behave when those products are quoted, sold, and serviced.").
