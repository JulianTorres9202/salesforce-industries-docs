# Remove a Coverage from a Commercial Quote

You can remove a coverage from a commercial quote if it is no longer needed.

1.  Click the arrow to the left of the insured item or party to expand the quote line item.
    
    Any optional coverages that can be removed have a **Remove** button at the right.
    
2.  Click the **Remove** button next to the coverage you're removing.
