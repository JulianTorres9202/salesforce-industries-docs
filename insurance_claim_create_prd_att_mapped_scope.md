# Create Product Mapped Scopes

Map the Product Attribute Scope to their respective root product attributes before creating a quote and issuing an insurance policy.

1.  From the Product Catalog Management app's home page, click **Products**.
2.  From the product list view page, click the policy root product. For example, Auto Silver.
3.  Navigate to the Attribute Mapping tab.
4.  Click **Assign**.
5.  Select the product attribute.
6.  Click **Next**.
7.  From the product attribute scope list view, select the product attribute scope.
8.  Save your changes.
