# Add Conditions to Taxes and Fees

Some taxes and fees may only be applicable to a quote or policy under certain conditions. To make this work, you can add conditions to taxes and fees after you've applied them to root products and child specs.

1.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)In the **Condition** field, enter the first part of the expression.
    
    [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)The type-ahead feature shows all the available options as you enter the start of the attribute code that will define this expression.
    
2.  Enter an operator.
3.  [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Enter the literal or function that completes the condition.
