# Manage Policies with Durations of Less Than a Year

Insurance carriers can expand their businesses by issuing policies with durations of less than 365 or 366 days. This change also supports endorsements, cancellations, renewals, reinstatements, and out-of-sequence endorsements on original policy versions with durations of less than a year.

**Where:** This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package and the Insurance Industries Extension managed package.
