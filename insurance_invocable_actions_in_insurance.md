# Invocable Actions in Insurance

Use these invocable actions in Insurance flows.

-   **[Create Accounts Invocable Action](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_create_accounts_invocable_action.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Create person account records of the specified type for group census members.
-   **[Create Contacts Invocable Action](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_create_contacts_invocable_action.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Creates contact records for group census members.
-   **[Create Portal Users Invocable Action](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_create_portal_users_invocable_action.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Creates user records with the IsPortalUser property enabled for group census members.
-   **[Enroll Members Invocable Action](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_enroll_members_invocable_action.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Creates insurance policies for specified primary members and their dependents by enrolling them into subscribed plans.
-   **[Generate User Inputs Invocable Action](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_generate_user_inputs_invocable_action.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Generates user inputs and fact ratings.
-   **[Rate Products Invocable Action](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_rate_products_invocable_action.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Rates insurance products as part of the Group Benefits Rating business process.
