# Analyze the Customer Experience with Your Service Support

Know the current sentiment of an insurance customer based on past engagements with the Contact Center and the sentiment recorded for those engagements.

**Where**: This change applies to Lightning Experience in Professional, Enterprise, and Unlimited editions with the Insurance Industries managed package.

**Who**: Sentiment Indicator is available to users who are assigned the Industry Service Excellence permission set and the FSC Insurance permission set.
