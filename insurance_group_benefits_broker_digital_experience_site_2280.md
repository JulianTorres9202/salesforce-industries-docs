# Group Benefits Broker Digital Experience Site

Your network of agents and brokers who sell your insurance products need access to specific features and functions to service their accounts. The Broker Experience site on the Experience Cloud gives them that access.

**Where:** This feature is available in the Insurance Application Suite under Group Voluntary Benefits Application.

**Who:** This feature is available to agents and admin users.

**Why:** Your brokers can have access to specific features and functions needed to service their accounts, all from one place.

**How:** We've included an out-of-the-box broker Experience Site with your Group Voluntary Benefits Application org. You can modify or extend this site to align it with your specific requirements.
