| To use benefit eligibility definitions: | Insurance Policy Management User |

1.  From the Object Management settings for **insurance benefits eligibility definition** object, go to **Fields & Relationships**.
2.  Click **Employee Type**.
3.  In the Employee Type Picklist Values section, click **New**.
4.  Add your desired picklist values.
5.  Save your work.
6.  Similarly, add picklist values to the Eligibility Initiation Type and Eligibility Termination Type field picklists.
