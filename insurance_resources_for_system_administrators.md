# Resources for System Administrators

Explore the services catalog, Apex triggers, UI templates, and more.

-   **[Insurance Services Catalog](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_insurance_and_health_insurance_services_catalog.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Get quote, policy, and claims systems up and running by using an extensive catalog of Digital Insurance Platform services. The services are methods of Apex classes that carry out common actions for insurance companies and health plans. They help you build end-to-end business processes faster by reducing or eliminating the need for custom programming.
-   **[Invocable Actions in Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_invocable_actions_in_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use these invocable actions in Insurance flows.
-   **[Insurance Apex Triggers](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_insurance_system_administration_597550.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Depending on how you use Insurance, you might need to perform additional tasks after installation, such as creating and modifying Custom Settings.
-   **[Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package Experience Templates](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_digital_experience_sites_644267.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use Experience site templates and pre-built components to start creating your own Experience sites. These are a combination of standard Salesforce Experience components, a few custom components used elsewhere in Insurance, plus a number of custom components we've created especially for Experience site.
-   **[Insurance Industries Extension Integration](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_insurance_industries_extension_integration_650361.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Enable the Insurance Industries Extension package and learn about a mapping interface that lets Vlocity policy services work with the Salesforce FSC Insurance Policy data model.
-   **[Insurance and Financial Services Data Model](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_vlocity_insurance_and_financial_services_data_model_651683.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Insurance and Financial Services data model is a comprehensive data model designed to address the complexities and challenges faced by cloud-based insurance and financial services systems across insurance markets.
