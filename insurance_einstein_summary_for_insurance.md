# Einstein Summary for Insurance

Empower your customer service representatives (CSRs) or captive agents to quickly generate a summary of the information stored in Account or Claim records by using Einstein Summary. The summary provides key details such as the policy name and type, standard premium, coverage details, or claim details. This feature improves operational efficiency and reduces response times. As a result, customer satisfaction increases, leading to better business outcomes.

To view the summary, drag the Einstein Summary component to a record page and configure the attributes. Einstein Summary uses template-triggered prompt flows to retrieve grounding data from object records. The prompt templates used are:

-   Summarize Insurance Account
