# Producer Role Assignments

Assign or reassign the producer role at account level and then inherit it to the policy for easy maintenance. Update the producer role for multiple accounts or policies in one go. The producer role assignment is applicable only when you use producer role split arrangement definitions.

-   **[Assign Producer Role to an Account](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_producer_split_role_assign_account.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Assign a role to producers at account level.
-   **[Assign Producer Role to a Policy](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_producer_split_role_assign_policy.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_brokerage_producer_split_role_assign_policy)**  
    Assign a role to producers at policy level either from an account record or outside of an account.
-   **[Reassign Producer Roles](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_producer_split_role_reassign.htm&language=en_US&type=5#insurance_brokerage_producer_split_role_reassign)**  
    Reassign producer roles to accounts or policies when the original producer is no longer associated with the account.
-   **[Update Producer for Multiple Accounts or Policies](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_producer_split_update_multiple.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Replace an existing producer for the accounts and policies with a new producer.
