# Set Up Coverages as Children of Insured Items for Multi-Instance

After you add coverages to a root product spec, you can associate them with an insured item.

1.  On the Coverages tab, go to the coverage you want to work with.
2.  In the Parent Insured Item, open the menu and select the insured item you want this coverage to be attached to.
    
    Note
    
    You can't associate coverages with an insured item or insured party that's a child of another insured item.
    
    Your associations save automatically.
