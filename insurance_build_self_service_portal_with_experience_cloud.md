# Insurance Policyholder Portal

With Experience Cloud, you can personalize the digital experience for your policyholders. Make it easy for policyholders to access their policies and take quick actions, as needed.

-   **[Learn About the Insurance Policyholder Portal](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_financial_services_client_portal_for_fsc_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    The Insurance Policyholder Portal is a self-service portal that empowers policyholders to manage their insurance accounts conveniently and independently. On this portal, your policyholders can access policy details, review coverage options, submit claims, and track the progress of the claims from the comfort of their devices. The portal offers transparency and efficiency in managing their insurance matters. The portal layouts are designed to primarily cater to the personal lines insurance use cases.
-   **[Set Up the Insurance Policyholder Portal](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_create_the_financial_services_client_portal_for_fsc_insurance.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Set up the Insurance Policyholder Portal using the Financial Services template in Experience Cloud and configure the self-service capabilities.
