# Large Group Quote LWC Experience Site User Permissions

To help your users create large group quotes quickly, add the Large Group Quote LWC to Experience Sites.

To provide Carrier Admin and HR Admin Experience Site users access to the Large Group Quote LWC, assign the [Insurance Create/Update Group Census Data Permission Set](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_insurance_create_update_group_census_data_permission_set.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Give your agents access to just what they need to create and update group census data.") and the [Insurance Create/Update Group Census Quote Permission Set](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_insurance_create_update_group_census_quote_permission_set.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Give your agents access to just what they need to create and update group census quotes.").
