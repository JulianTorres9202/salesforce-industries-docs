# Plan Your Upgrade to Insurance

Before you upgrade, verify a few details about your current org and figure out exactly which release to upgrade to.

-   **[Gather and Verify Info About Your Insurance Org](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_gather_and_verify_org_info_asof242.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Before you upgrade, do some preliminary work in your current org. These tasks inform your decision about an upgrade path, and prepare you for extra steps that can apply to your upgrade.
-   **[Choose an Upgrade Path for Insurance](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_choose_an_upgrade_path_246.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Insurance releases are cumulative. That is, the Winter ’25 major release contains everything from the Summer ’24 major release and all of the contents of the Summer ’24 minor releases. Plus, it has all of the new features and enhancements for Winter ’25.
