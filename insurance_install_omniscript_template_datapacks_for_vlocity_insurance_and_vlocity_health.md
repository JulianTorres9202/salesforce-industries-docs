# Install OmniScript Template DataPacks for Insurance

OmniScript templates control the look, feel, and some of the behavior of Insurance OmniScripts.

1.  From the App Launcher, open **OmniStudio Templates**.
2.  Click the down arrow, then select one of the templates listed below.
3.  On the **Select Items to Import** window, click **Next**, then click **Next** again.
4.  On the **Import Complete** window, click **Activate Now**.
5.  On the **Select Items to Activate** window, view the list of templates to activate and make sure that everything is selected.
    
    Tip
    
    If you've customized any templates and want to continue using your custom versions, deselect those templates in this list.
    
6.  Click **Next**, then click **Done**.
7.  Repeat steps 2-6 for each template.
