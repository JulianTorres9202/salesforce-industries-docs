# Automatic Upgrades

Starting in Spring '23, we're moving our Insurance managed package to the Salesforce automatic upgrade process. This process aligns with the Salesforce seasonal release cycle, where new features are delivered three times a year (Summer, Winter, and Spring). With automatic upgrades, you also get early access to upcoming releases in your sandbox preview environment.

**Where:** This feature is available starting in Spring '23. Automatic upgrades are required starting in Spring '24.

**Who:** All Insurance licenses.

**Why:** Automatic upgrades keep your sandboxes and production environments current with the latest features, let you test customizations and new features in your sandboxes, and provide regular patch updates with performance, stability, and security improvements.

**How:** To receive automatic upgrades, opt in as described in the [Salesforce Industries Automatic Upgrade](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=000391836&language=en_us&type=1) Knowledge Article.
