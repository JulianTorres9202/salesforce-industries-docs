# Cancel Policy Business Process

The policy cancellation business process lets brokers and policyholders cancel an in-force policy. The policyholder receives a refund for the unutilized premium on the policy, which is calculated based on the policy effective date and the effective date of the cancellation transaction.

-   **[Cancel Policy OmniScript](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_cancel_policy_omniscript_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    You can launch this OmniScript from the following applications, where it can be accessed by the types of users described:
-   **[Cancel Policy from Policies UI](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_cancel_policy_from_policies_omnistudio.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    To cancel any policy from the Policies UI, first go to the Policies list page and click the policy you want to cancel.
