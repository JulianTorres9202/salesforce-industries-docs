# Create a Commercial Auto Quote Using OmniScript

Add details to create a quote for a Commercial Auto Policy with multiple vehicles associated with multiple locations.

1.  From an Account page, click **Commercial Auto Quote**.
    
    Note The Commercial Auto Quote is available only if the record type of the account is a Business Record.
    
2.  In the Add Location window, click **New**.
3.  Enter the location details in the respective fields and then click **Save**.
    
    To add more locations, click **New** and repeat the previous step.
    
4.  Click **Next**.
5.  In the Add Your Vehicles window, click **New**.
6.  Enter the vehicle details and then click **Save**.
    
    To add vehicles to more locations, click **New** and repeat the previous step.
    
7.  Click **Next**.
8.  View the details of the locations, vehicles associated with each location, and the total premium for each location.
9.  View the premium and update coverages for each vehicle associated with a location, and then click **Next**.
10.  Review the location, vehicle, and premium details for one last time, and then click **Next**.
     
     A confirmation window with quote details and a **View Quote** button appears.
     
11.  Click **View Quote**.
     
     The Quote page is displayed.
