# Add a Grandchild Insured Party or Insured Item to an Insured Item

If you're working with a multi-instance insurance product with grandchild insured items or insured parties, you can add a grandchild to an existing insured item.

For example, you can add a driver to a car on a quote.

1.  On the insured item you're adding to, click the **Add** Item or Party button.
    
    Example: If you are adding a driver (a grandchild insured party) to a car (the insured item) on an auto quote, you can click the **\+ Driver** button.
    
2.  Select **Create New**.
3.  In the **Add** Item or Party window, fill out the appropriate information, and then click **Save**.
