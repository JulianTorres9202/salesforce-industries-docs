# Associate a Product Class to an Existing Root Product Spec

When you associate a product class to an existing root product spec, the root product spec inherits all the coverages, insured items, insured parties, attributes, and rules from the parent class you select. These items add to whatever items are already present in the root product spec. They do not replace similar or identical items.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Tip

Vlocity recommends you go through your root product spec after you associate a product class. Make sure you don't have any duplicate items.

If you do have duplicate items inherited from the product class, delete the original (not inherited) versions.

1.  Open the root product spec and go to the **Details** tab.
2.  At the top right, click **Edit**.
    
    You may need to click the down-arrow at the top right to find **Edit**.
    
3.  On the **Edit** window, go to **Parent Class**.
4.  Find the parent class in the list and choose it.
5.  Click **Save**.
