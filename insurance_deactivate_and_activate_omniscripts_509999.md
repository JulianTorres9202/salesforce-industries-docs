# Deactivate and Activate OmniScripts

Log in to your org and then deactivate and activate OmniScripts so they can run as intended.

1.  In the OmniStudio OmniScript Designer, find the OmniScript and open it.
2.  Click **Deactivate Version**. This will take a couple of minutes.
3.  Click **Done**.
4.  Now click **Activate Version**. Again, this will take about a minute to complete.
5.  Click **Done**. That's it!
