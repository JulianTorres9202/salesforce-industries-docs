# Overview of a Claim

Claims adjusters can see essential details about a claim at a glance.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Details

The **Details** tab of a claim contains specifics about the claim object, including automatically generated information like the claim number, the date the loss was reported, the date of the loss, and the date an adjuster was assigned.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Note

The Vlocity administrator sets up the fields that appear on the Details tab. See [Customize the Claims Adjuster's View of Claim Details](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_customize_the_claims_adjuster_s_view_of_claim_details_619387.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Administrators configure page layouts with information that helps adjusters assess claims at a glance.").

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Available Coverages

The **Coverages** tab shows the coverages available in the in-force policy. This read-only information comes from the policy record.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Note

This tab doesn't show open claim coverages. To see and work with claim coverages, go to the **Financials** tab.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Claim Participants and Involved Items

The **Claim Participants** tab shows all the participants involved in a claim, including participant details, involved injuries, and involved properties.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)To learn how administrators can set up this tab, see [Configure the Claim Participant UI](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_configure_the_claim_participant_ui_619243.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "The LWC Claim Participant UI shows information about claim participants and involved items associated with each participant. It lets the claims adjuster add, edit, and delete claim participants and the involved items associated with the participants.").

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)To learn how to add new involved properties, people (claim relationships), and other items to a claim, see [Manage a Claim's Participants](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_manage_a_claim_s_participants_621036.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "As a claims adjuster, you have access to essential information about claim participants and can complete tasks related to them.") and [Manage a Claim's Involved Items](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_manage_a_claim_s_involved_items_621092.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "As a claims adjuster, you can see at a glance which of a claim's involved items relate to each claim participant. You can also complete tasks related to involved items. For example, you can associate a claim participant who's a passenger in an auto accident with the vehicle they were riding in.").

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Claim History

The **History** tab shows a timeline of claim activity in a single, chronological feed that's easy to scan and drill into. See essential activity at a glance and expand content like notes and emails to dive into more detail.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)A DataPack included with your org gets you started. It has story object configuration that brings essential content into a Claim History tab out of the box. To learn more, see [Configure Insurance History with Claim History LWC DataPack](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_configure_insurance_history_with_claim_history_lwc_datapack_620180.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "You can configure the Insurance History Lightning web component to create a cohesive view of all things happening with a claim. A DataPack included with your org gets you started. It has story object configuration that brings essential content into a Claim History tab out of the box.").

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Related Info

The **Related** tab contains links to records associated with this claim such as Claim Coverages, Claim Participants, and Claim Items.
