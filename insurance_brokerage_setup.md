# Set Up Brokerage

Manage the setup and system configurations for Policy Management and Commissions Management to suit your business requirements.

It is mandatory to turn on Brokerage preferences to use the Brokerage features. After turning on the preferences, you can customize the Policy Management and Commissions Management experiences. You can't turn off these preferences after you enable them. Before you turn on Brokerage preferences, ensure to [turn on context service](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.turn_on_context_service.htm&language=en_US&type=5).

-   **[Set Up Policy Management](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_setup_policy.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Manage all the preferences and configurations related to policy management.
-   **[Set Up Commission Management](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_setup_commission.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Manage all the configurations related to commission management.
