# Policyholder Digital Experience Site

Give your policyholders access to view and change important information about their account, policies, and billing options with the Policyholder Digital Experience site.

The Policyholder site contains a bunch of components to get you started with providing your policyholders with functionality they can use to self-manage some parts of their policies. These components are made with FlexCards that you can customize and activate to compile Lightning web components for use on the site. For more information on the components, see the [Policyholder Digital Experience Site Component Catalog](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policyholder_digital_experience_site_component_catalog_645782.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "The Policyholder site contains a bunch of components to get you started with providing your policyholders with functionality they can use to self-manage some parts of their policies.").

Note Digital Experience templates don't include features from Winter '24 onwards.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## What Your User Can See in the Policyholder Site

The Policyholder site template contains two top-level pages:

-   Home
    
-   Policies
    

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Home

When a policyholder logs into their Policyholder site, they land on the Home page.

On this page, your users can:

-   See the plans they are enrolled in and view a statement for these plans.
    
    When the enrollment window is open, users also have the option to enroll in a plan from here.
    
-   See a list of their policies.
-   See contact information for their agent
-   Update account information

Note Remember, you must customize these components for your own Policyholder site.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Policies

Your Policyholder site users have a policy record page for each of their policies.

On this page, your users can see all the policies they hold. They can click each policy to see its overview.

Note Remember, you must customize these components for your own Policyholder site.
