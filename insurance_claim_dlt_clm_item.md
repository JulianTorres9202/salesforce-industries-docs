# Delete a Claim Item

Delete the items that are duplicates or irrelevant to a claim participant.

1.  From the App Launcher, find and select **Claims**.
2.  Select a claim from the list view.
3.  Navigate to the Participants tab and expand the claim participant that you want to delete the claim item for.
4.  Click the dropdown menu of the claim item that you want to delete, and select **Delete**.
5.  Click **Delete**.
