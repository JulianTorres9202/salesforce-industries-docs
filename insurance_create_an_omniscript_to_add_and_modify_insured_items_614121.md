# Create an OmniScript to Add and Modify Insured Items

Create a Lightning web component-based OmniScript for your users to use when they add and modify insured items to quotes.

Important

Activate your OmniScript before you add it to the Quote UI.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)For details about creating the OmniScript, see [Create an LWC OmniScript](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=xcloud.os_create_an_lwc_omniscript.htm&language=en_US&type=5).

After you've activated your OmniScript, get its markup to add to the Lightning web component later in this process.

1.  Open your OmniScript in the OmniScript Designer.
2.  On the middle pane, select Script Configuration.
3.  At the top right, click **How to launch activated script**.
4.  Click the **LWC** tab.
5.  Under **Embedded**, click **Copy to clipboard** next to the topmost line of code.
6.  Click **Ok** to dismiss the **How to Launch** window.
