# Get to Know the Workflow for Group Benefits Bulk Enrollment with Census Management

The Enrollment Census Lightning web component gives insurance carrier administrators a straightforward way to upload group benefits product enrollment data with census members and dependents.

-   **[Upload Group Census Data](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_upload_group_census_data_and_bulk_enroll_members.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    After a contract is in place, upload group benefits product enrollment data with census members and dependents.
-   **[Search for Large Group Census Members](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_search_for_large_group_cecnsus_members.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Quickly find census member details during large group enrollment and quoting by using the Search box in the Enrollment Census Lightning Web component and Quote Census Lightning web component respectively.
-   **[Add a New Census Member or Update Details of Existing Members](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_add_a_new_census_member_or_update_details_of_existing_members.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Add a new primary member or a dependent to the existing census during bulk enrollment. You can also update the details of existing census members.
-   **[View the Plan Coverage Details of Members for Enrollment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_view_the_plan_coverage_details_of_members_for_enrollment.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    After you upload group benefits coverage selections of members, view the coverage selections for a member.
-   **[Bulk Enroll Members](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_bulk_enroll_members.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    You can bulk enroll up to 10,000 census members into their selected plans and policies.
