# Set a Primary Grandchild Insured Party or Insured Item

Some quotes require a primary grandchild insured item or insured party. You can set the primary insured item or insured party to give your customers just the right coverage.

1.  On the insured item, click the name of the grandchild insured party or insured item.
2.  Select **Set as Primary**.
    
    An asterisk appears next to the name of the insured item or insured party you just selected as primary (and disappears from the previous primary).
