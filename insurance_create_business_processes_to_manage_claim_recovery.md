# Create Business Processes to Manage Claim Recovery

Use the Claim Recovery object in the Salesforce data model along with `InsClaimRecoveryService:getClaimRecoveries` and `InsClaimRecoveryService:createUpdateClaimRecoveries` services. An end-to-end process maintains a complete history of claim recovery efforts and shows how recoveries affect overall financial exposure over time.

-   **[Add the Claim Recoveries Related List to the Claim Page](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_add_the_claim_recoveries_related_list_to_the_claim_page.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Claim team members manage claim recovery efforts by using the Claim Recoveries related list.
-   **[Pursue and Accept Claim Recovery Amounts](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_pursue_and_accept_claim_recovery_amounts.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    As team members pursue, accept, and seek additional recovery amounts, they track each step of the claim recovery process.
