# Disassociate a Product Class from a Root Product Spec

When you disassociate a product class from a root product spec, the inherited items from the product class are automatically removed from the root product spec.

1.  Open the root product spec and go to the **Details** tab.
2.  At the top right, click **Edit**.
    
    You may need to click the down-arrow at the top right to find Edit.
    
3.  On the **Edit** window, go to **Parent Class**.
4.  Delete the parent class from the field.
5.  Click **Save**.
