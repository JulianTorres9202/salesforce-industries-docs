# Set Up Your Digital Experience Site

Create an Experience site for your users using the templates provided in the Insurance FSC unmanaged package.

The Insurance FSC unmanaged package contains templates that you can customize. We recommend that you uninstall the unmanaged package immediately after you save and assign your own template.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

## Workflow for Creating Your Digital Experience site

Here's the recommended flow for setting up your Experience site:

To set up your Digital Experience site, follow the diagram above. Each step in the diagram has its own topic:

-   [Install the Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_install_the_insurance_fsc_unmanaged_package_650038.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "To start using templates to create an Experience site for your users, install the Insurance FSC unmanaged package.")
    
-   [Create Your Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_create_your_experience_site_650072.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "To start using the templates provided in the unmanaged package, create an Experience site for your users.")
    
-   [Update the Experience Site CSS](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_update_the_experience_site_css_650113.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Update the Experience site CSS to ensure that buttons and links display correctly.")
    
-   [Hide or Show Components for Mobile](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_hide_or_show_components_for_mobile_650158.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Some components are designed specifically for mobile devices. To ensure a seamless experience for your desktop and mobile users, choose to hide or show components depending on the type of device they are using.")
    
-   [Save Your Experience Site Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_save_your_experience_site_template_650229.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Save a template so that you can customize the Experience site for your users. This step is necessary so that you can uninstall the unmanaged package.")
    
-   [Assign Your Template to Your Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_assign_your_template_to_your_experience_site_650263.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Assign the template you created to your Experience site and start customizing it to deliver a unique experience to your users.")
    
-   [Uninstall the Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_uninstall_the_insurance_fsc_unmanaged_package_650325.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Uninstall the Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package so that you are ready to install future versions of this package when they are released.")
