# Create a Product Classification

Create a product classification across benefit specifications. This way, you can create a single product classification that includes all the attributes you need, and then use that product classification to create multiple benefit specifications.

Use this procedure to create all product classifications. For example, OOP Limit, Preventive Care, Chiropractic Care, and Rx Mail-order that are listed in the [Plan Benefits Sample Information](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_brokerage_product_model_sample_benefits_information.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Use the plan benefits information to model the medical, dental, and vision coverage products under the Employee Benefits root product.") topic.

1.  From the Product Catalog Management Home page, select **Product Classification**, and then click **New**.
2.  Enter the required information for Annual Deductible product classification and save your changes.
    
    When entering the product classification details, enter a unique code and make sure you activate the product.
