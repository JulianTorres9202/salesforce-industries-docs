# Modify Attributes on Insured Items and Insured Parties

You can change the configuration of any attribute in an insured item or insured party on a root product spec. Your changes to an insured item or party on a root product override the inherited configuration from the insured item spec.

1.  On the **Insured Items** tab, click the carat next to the attribute category name to open the list of attributes.
2.  On the attribute line you want to modify, change any of the following:
    
    -   **Rating**
        
    -   **Rating Attribute Use**
        
    -   **Rating Mapping**
        
    -   **Rules**
        
    
    Your modifications get saved automatically.
