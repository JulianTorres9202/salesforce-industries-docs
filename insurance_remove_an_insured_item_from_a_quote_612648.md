# Remove an Insured Item from a Quote

You can remove an insured item from a quote to provide your customer with the necessary coverage when their needs change.

1.  Click the **Remove** button to the right of the insured item premium.
2.  On the **Delete Quote Line Item** window, click **Delete**.
