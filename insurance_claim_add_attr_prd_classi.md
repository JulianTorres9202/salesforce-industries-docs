# Assign Attributes to a Product Classification

After you create a Product Classification, assign attributes to it. You can assign individual attributes to a product classification or a collection of attributes through attribute categories.

To assign attributes to Auto Claim Class, Involved Injury Class, and Damaged Property Class, follow these instructions:

1.  From the Product Catalog Management app's home page, click **Product Classifications**.
2.  On the Product Classifications list view page, click the product classification to which you want to assign attributes.
3.  Navigate to the Attributes tab.
4.  In the Attributes section, click **Assign**.
5.  To assign individual attributes to the product classification, select **Assign individual attributes**.
6.  Click **Next**.
7.  If you selected Assign individual attributes, then select one or more attributes from the Categorized and Uncategorized tabs and click **Assign**.
    
    You can assign only active attributes to product classifications.
