# Commercial Application Overview

The Commercial Application provides a fully functional example of Commercial Auto, Essential Business and General Liability product models, rating procedures, and business processes for quoting, and issuing, policies.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)This Application can be used for reference or as the base for the development of product models and business processes. This guide will orient you to the contents of the org and the business processes for commercial insurance. We've included these products and processes in the Property and Casualty org available in the Insurance Process Library.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)
