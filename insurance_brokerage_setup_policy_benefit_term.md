# Define the Benefit Term for Line of Business

Add and update the benefit term for a line of business.

| To set up brokerage: | Insurance Brokerage User |

1.  In Setup, find and select **Brokerage**.
2.  Select **Policy Management**, and then select **Coverage Benefits**.
3.  In the Insurance Policy Line of Business Configuration section, click **Add**.
4.  Select the Line of Business and enter a term name.
5.  Save your work.
6.  To update the term name, double-click the term name and update it.
7.  Save your changes.
