# Auto Quote from Policyholder Site

Policyholders can follow a guided flow to quickly create quotes from Auto insurance.

For details of the quoting flow for Commercial Auto products, see the [Commercial quoting flow](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_commercial_quote_business_process_1.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "We've created a quote business process for General Liability for you to examine, use as an example, and extend to create your own business processes for insurance.").

All our screenshots are currently in Lightning style. Your spin may look different because it's using the Newport style in the Broker Portal and Customer Portal.

1.  From the policyholder site, click **Auto**.
2.  Click **Next**.
3.  Some of your details are already filled in but make sure you check them. Select a Start Date for the policy and click **Next**.
    
    Tip
    
    To navigate through this flow quickly using seed data, click **Autofill** at the bottom of the form.
    
4.  Select the drivers you want to insure and click **Next**. To add more drivers, click **New**.
5.  Select relevant options for drivers to avail a discounted rate and click **Next**.
6.  To add details of the vehicles you want to insure, click **New**.
    
    When you're done, click **Next**.
    
7.  You see a quote for your vehicle. To modify coverages, click **Customize coverage details**.
8.  Click **Edit Auto Driver**. Now select the map driver or drivers to the vehicles as primary or driver only.
    
    When you're done, click **Next**.
    
9.  That's it! Your quote is created and you get a confirmation. To see the summary of quote details, click **View Quote**.
    
    You can see the created quote on your Home page as well.
