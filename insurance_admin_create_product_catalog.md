# Create a Product Catalog for Digital Insurance

Define your product offerings in Product Catalog Management with product catalogs. Catalogs enable efficient organization and management of all the products your business offers.

### Required Editions

1.  Create a product catalog to organize your insurance products. For example, you might have separate product catalogs for auto, home, and pet insurance products. See [Create a Catalog](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.product_catalog_create_a_catalog.htm&language=en_US&type=5).
2.  Create product categories to segment and manage your products. For example, within an auto insurance catalog, you might have categories for commercial and personal insurance products. See [Create Catalog Categories and Subcategories](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.product_catalog_create_catalog_categories_and_subcategories.htm&language=en_US&type=5).
3.  Assign the products in your offering to the appropriate categories. See [Assign Products to Catalog Categories in Revenue Cloud](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.product_catalog_assign_products_to_catalog_cat_and_subcategories.htm&language=en_US&type=5).
4.  To make product and product category data available to your international users in their language, set up data translation. See [Set Up Data Translation in Product Catalog Management](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.product_catalog_set_up_data_translation_in_product_catalog_management.htm&language=en_US&type=5).
