# Customize Views of Claim Participants Based on Roles

Help claims adjusters focus on what's important for each person or party involved in a claim. Show unique field values and actions for drivers, passengers, repair shops, and other participant roles.

-   **[Configure Field Sets for Claim Participant Roles](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_configure_field_sets_for_claim_participant_roles.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Show claims adjusters exactly the right information for each claim participant, nothing extra. For example, configure separate field sets to show different information for first-party claimants, third-party claimants, and auto body shops.
-   **[Configure Actions for Claim Participant Roles](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_configure_actions_for_claim_participant_roles.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Customize actions that apply to each claim participant role. For example, show the Refresh Credit Score action for all claimants, and the OFAC Search action only for first-party claimants.
