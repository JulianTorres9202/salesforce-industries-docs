# Create Claim Involved Injury Specs and Claim Involved Property Specs

Claim injury specs and claim property specs are parts of a claim products.

1.  On the **Products** list page, click **New**.
2.  On the **New Product** page, choose **Claim Injury Spec** or **Claim Property Spec**, then click **Next**.
3.  Fill out the details for this spec, then click **Save**.
4.  On the **Attributes** tab, click **New** to add attributes to the spec. Add all the attributes that apply to this spec.
5.  Configure each attribute.
