# Insurance Property & Casualty Application Suite Producer Interaction

When a producer contacts a service agent they can verify the broker or producer detail.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)After receiving a call from a broker:

1.  Open the Interaction Launcher from the utility bar at the bottom.
2.  In the **AGENT** section, enter any or all of the required information about the broker and click **Search**.
3.  The results are displayed and you need to click **Verify** to corroborate customer information.
4.  Based on the agent's responses, click the checkmark to verify relevant details and then click **Verify & Search**.
5.  Next, enter policy information and click **Search**.
6.  Click **View** to see the interaction console for the policy you are interested in.
