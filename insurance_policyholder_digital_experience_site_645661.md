# Policyholder Digital Experience Site

Give your policyholders access to view and change important information about their account, policies, claims, and billing options with the Policyholder Digital Experience site.

We provide you with a site template and a set of components to get you started with creating your own Policyholder site. These are a combination of standard Salesforce Experience components, a few custom components used elsewhere in Vlocity Insurance, plus a number of custom components we've created especially for the Policyholder site.

Note

To use the Policyholder site template, you must install the [Insurance FSC Unmanaged Package](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_ind_ins_release_summary.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Insurance includes managed packages, unmanaged packages, and an Installation Assistant that helps you complete installation and upgrade tasks.").

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

You can brand the components with your colors, fonts, logo, and other styles. You can also customize the substance of the components by adding or removing data, changing the data source, adding new elements, and more. To learn how, see Digital Experience Site Component Customization Overview.

Important

We recommend that you use the Experience Builder to create and save your own custom template for your Policyholder site. To learn more, see [Save Your Experience Site Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_save_your_experience_site_template_650229.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Save a template so that you can customize the Experience site for your users. This step is necessary so that you can uninstall the unmanaged package.").

Keep reading to check out what you can do with the out-of-the-box Policyholder site

## What Your User Can See in the Policyholder Site

The Policyholder site template contains top-level pages:

1.  [Policyholder Digital Experience Site Home Page](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policyholder_digital_experience_site_home_page.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "When a policyholder logs into their Policyholder site, they land on the Home page.")
    
2.  [Policyholder Digital Experience Site Policies Page](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policyholder_digital_experience_site_policies_page.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Your Policyholder site users have a policy record page for each of their policies.")
    
3.  [Policyholder Digital Experience Site Billing Page](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policyholder_digital_experience_site_billing_page.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "The billing page provides your Policyholder Experience users with lots of information about their billing account.")
    
4.  [Policyholder Digital Experience Site Claims Page](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_policyholder_digital_experience_site_claims_page.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "The Claims page shows your Policyholder site users a list of claim records associated with their account.
