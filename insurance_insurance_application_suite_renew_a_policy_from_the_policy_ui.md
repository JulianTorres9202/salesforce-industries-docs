# Insurance Application Suite Renew a Policy from the Policy UI

A customer service rep or internal insurance agent with access to the Policy UI can renew policies from that UI.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

1.  If a policy has been up for renewal within the last 30 days or will be up for renewal in the next 30 days, you see the **Renew Policy** option under **Actions** in the policy record.
    
    From **Actions**, click **Renew Policy**.
    
2.  Next, you can modify the coverages if you'd like. When you're done, click **Next**.
3.  That's really all there is to it! The policy is renewed. To see the new policy, click **View Policy**.
