# Get to Know the Workflow for Group Benefits Individual Enrollment

Give group members a straightforward way for self enrollment into selected plans from the customer portal.

-   **[Enroll Yourself and Your Dependents Into Selected Plans](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_enroll_yourself_and_dependents_into_selected_plans.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    After a customer views and selects one or more eligible plans, they can rate and enroll themselves into the selected plans.
-   **[Services for Individual Enrollment](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_services_for_individual_enrollment.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Use these services to configure the workflow for individual enrollment, or use custom implementation according to your business requirement.
