# Add a New Census Member or Update Details of Existing Members

Add a new primary member or a dependent to the existing census during bulk enrollment. You can also update the details of existing census members.

-   **[Add a New Primary Member with Plans](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_add_a_new_primary_member_with_plans.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    You can add a new primary member who has not opted for any plans.
-   **[Add a New Primary Member Without Any Plans](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_add_a_new_primary_member_without_any_plans.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    You can add a new primary member who hasn’t opted for any plans.
-   **[Add a Dependent Member to the Primary Member](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_add_a_dependent_member_to_the_primary_member.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    You can add a dependent member to an existing primary member in the census.
-   **[Update the Information of the Census Member](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_update_member_information.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    You can update personal information, plan, and coverage selections of any member in the census during bulk enrollment.
