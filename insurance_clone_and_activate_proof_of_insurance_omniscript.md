# Clone and Activate the Proof of Insurance Omniscript

Clone and activate the FSCIns/RequestInsuranceProof OmniScript. This Omniscript gives your users a guided path for requesting the insurance proof. Use the Omniscript as it is, or customize it to meet the needs of your business.

1.  From the App Launcher, find and select Omnistudio.
2.  In the Omnistudio app, from the navigation bar, select OmniScripts.
    
    It can take a little time for the Omniscripts app to open.
    
3.  If Standard Omnistudio Runtime is disabled, turn it on.
4.  Select **FSCIns/RequestInsuranceProof**.
5.  Click **New Version**.
6.  Click **Activate Version**.
