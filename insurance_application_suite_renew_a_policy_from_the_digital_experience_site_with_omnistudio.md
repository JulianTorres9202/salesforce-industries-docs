# Insurance Application Suite Renew a Policy from the Digital Experience Site

Policyholders and brokers can renew policies right from their Experience Sites.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Policyholders can directly renew their policies from the self-service policyholder Experience Site. When the policy is due for renewal, the user sees an option on the right. Clicking this initiates the Renew Policy OmniScript. Here's an example of what it looks like on the policyholder site for Business Owners.

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Similarly, brokers can also renew policies from the self-service broker Experience Site. When the policy is due for renewal, the user sees an option on the right. Clicking this initiates the Renew Policy OmniScript. Here's an example of what it looks like on the broker site for Business Owners.
