# Manage Complex Quotes and Policies with Commercial Quoting and Commercial Policy Lightning Web Components

Your users can easily view, configure, reprice, and navigate complex quotes and policies with multi-level insured item relationships and coverages with the commercial quoting and commercial policy LWCs.

**Where:** Available in Summer '22 and later releases.

**Why:** Commercial quotes and policies can have complex product structures with multiple levels of insured items. With the Insurance Commercial Quote, your users can add coverages and ratings to parent, child, and grandchild insured items. This gives your customers exactly the coverage that they need. The Insurance Commercial Quote LWC provides your users with a concise view of multi-root quotes. Users can expand and minimize quote line items at the product, insured item or party, or grandchild insured item level and can view details about coverages in the coverage detail side panel.

The Insurance Commercial Policy LWC provides users with the same expandable view and shows details about the policy at the parent, product, insured item or party, and grandchild insured item levels.
