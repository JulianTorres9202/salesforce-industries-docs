# Insurance Policy Lightning Web Component

The Insurance policy Lightning web component (LWC) gives captive agents quick access to important policy information directly on the Insurance Policy record.

Insurance policies can be complex, with multiple levels of policy line items such as coverages, assets, and participants. For example, an auto insurance policy typically lists the vehicle as the asset, accident protection as the coverage, and the driver as the participant.

On the Insurance Policy Lightning web component, your users can view:

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)

-   Policy hierarchy
-   Insured parties
-   Premium, taxes, and fees for each insured item

When users select a policy line item, the side panel shows the details of the line item such as the associated products, attributes, and their values.
