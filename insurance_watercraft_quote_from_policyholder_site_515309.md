# Watercraft Quote from Policyholder Site

Policyholders can follow a guided flow to quick create quotes for marine insurance.

All our screenshots are currently in Lightning style. Your spin may look different because it's using the Newport style in the Broker Portal and Customer Portal.

1.  From the policyholder site, click **Watercrafts**.
2.  Click **Get Quote**.
3.  Add details of the vessel you want to insure and click **Next**.
    
    Tip
    
    To navigate through this flow quickly using seed data, click **Auto Fill Vessels** at the bottom of the form.
    
4.  Enter details of the operator to be insured along with their boating experience and click **Next**.
5.  You see a quote for your vessel. To modify coverages, click **Customize coverage details**.
    
    When you're done, click **Next**.
    
    That's it! Your quote is created and you get a confirmation. To see the summary of quote details, click **View Quote**. You can see the created quote on your Home page as well.
