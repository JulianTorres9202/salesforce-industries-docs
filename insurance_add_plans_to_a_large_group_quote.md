# Add Plans to a Large Group Quote

To start configuring a large group quote, add a plan to it. You must add a plan to a quote before you can add coverages.

1.  From the Quote list page, open the quote you want to add a plan to.
2.  Click **Add Plan** on the Large Group Quote LWC.
3.  Select a plan or plans, and then click **Add**.
    
    Note You can rate up to 30 summary-based plans and up to 150,000 census members at a time. With member-based plans you can rate up to 30 plans and 3,000 members at a time.
