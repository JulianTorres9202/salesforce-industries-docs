# Insurance Coexistence

Use Digital Insurance alongside the Insurance managed package or Insurance CRM in your existing org. Launch new products faster by using Digital Insurance while you manage existing products, quotes, policies, and claims as before without immediately migrating them. Segregate the transactional data, metadata, and automations based on the originating solution even with shared data model objects. Avoid the administrative burden of managing separate orgs for each insurance solution and the related complexity in user management and reporting.

-   **[Insurance Coexistence Considerations](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_admin_coexistence_considerations.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Before you set up Insurance Coexistence, make sure that you review some key factors that affect your implementation.
-   **[Set Up Insurance Coexistence with Salesforce Go](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_admin_set_up_coexistence.htm&language=en_US&type=5)**  
    Configure your org for the coexistence of Digital Insurance with the Insurance manage package or Insurance CRM.
