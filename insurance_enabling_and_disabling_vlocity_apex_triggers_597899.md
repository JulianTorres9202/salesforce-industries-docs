# Enabling and Disabling Vlocity Apex Triggers

You can use Salesforce Custom Settings to enable or disable Vlocity Apex triggers.

1.  From **Setup**, in the **Quick Find** box, enter Custom Settings.
2.  Click **Custom Settings**.
3.  Click **T** to limit the results.
4.  Click **Manage** next to **Trigger Setup**.
5.  Click **Edit** next to the name of the trigger you want to enable or disable. If you don't see the trigger you want to enable or disable on the Trigger Setup page, you need to add the trigger.
    1.  On the **Trigger Setup** page, click **New**.
    2.  Enter the **Trigger Handler Name** of the trigger you are adding in the **Name** field.
    3.  Select the **Trigger On** box to enable the trigger. Deselect the box to disable the trigger.
    4.  Click **Save**.
6.  Select the **Trigger On** box to enable the trigger. Deselect the box to disable the trigger.
7.  Click **Save**.
