# Download and Install the FSCCustomFieldMap File

To get all of the great mappings you need for Insurance Industries Extension objects and fields, download and install the FSCCustomFieldMap file.

1.  From **Setup**, in the **Quick Find** box, enter Static Resources.
2.  Click **Static Resources**.
3.  Click **DP\_CUSTOM\_FSCCustomFieldMap**.
4.  Right-click on the **View File** link and select **Save Link As**.
5.  Add .JSON as an extension at the end of the filename suggested. Navigate to an easy place on your local computer to retrieve the file, then click **Save**.
6.  Back in your Vlocity org, from the App Launcher, open the **OmniStudio DataPacks** page.
7.  Click the **Installed** tab.
8.  Click **Import From** at the top right of the page, then select **From File**.
9.  Click **Browse**, then navigate to the **DP\_CUSTOM\_FSCCustomFieldMap.JSON** file on your local computer.
10.  Click **Open**, then click **Next**.
11.  On the **Select Items to Import** window, make sure all the items are selected, then click **Next**.
12.  Click **Next** again. When the import is complete, click **Done**.
