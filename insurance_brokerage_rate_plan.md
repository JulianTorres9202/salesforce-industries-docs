# Insurance Rate Plan

Rate plans represent the cost of the insurance policy for the client and are typically described in terms of premium or fees. However, in some scenarios, they are described in terms of fully insured equivalent premium.

Rates can be categorized by tiers which is a further breakdown of the type of coverage that is being purchased. For example, a tier can include only an employee, or an employee and their spouse, or an employee, their spouse, and their kids. Each tier typically has a different rate amount and enrollment counts associated with it, which are inputs to the overall premium, fee, or fully insured equivalent calculation. For more information on how cost is calculated, see [Cost Calculation](https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/atlas.en-us.insurance_developer_guide.meta/insurance_developer_guide/connect_resources_cost_calculation.htm).
