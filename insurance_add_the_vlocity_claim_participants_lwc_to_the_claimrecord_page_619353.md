# Add the Vlocity Claim Participants LWC to the Claim Record Page

After administrators configure the Vlocity Claim Participants LWC, they can add it to the Claim record page. Users can also add the Vlocity Claim Participants LWC to the page.

1.  Click the quick access menu (the gear icon) and select **Edit Page**.
2.  Click the Tabs component in the middle panel.
3.  Find the **Vlocity Claim Participants** component, then drag it to the Claim page so it's added as a tab.
4.  Click **Save**.
