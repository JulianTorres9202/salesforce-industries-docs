# Policyholder Digital Experience Site Billing Page

The billing page provides your Policyholder Experience users with lots of information about their billing account.

Out of the box, the Billing page looks like this:

On the Billing page, your users can:

-   See an overview of their billing account
    
-   Use links to make payments, view statements, and update their billing information
    
-   See a list of previous transactions on their billing account
    

[](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US)Note

Remember, you must customize these components for your own Policyholder site.
