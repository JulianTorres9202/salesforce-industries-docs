# Assign Your Template to Your Experience Site

Assign the template you created to your Experience site and start customizing it to deliver a unique experience to your users.

The highlighted area in this flow shows where you are in the process of setting up your site:

1.  On the Experience Builder page, click [](https://help.salesforce.com/s?language=en_US) then select **Workspaces**.
2.  Click the **Administration** tile.
3.  On the Settings tab, click **Change Template**.
4.  Select the template you saved in [Save Your Experience Site Template](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_save_your_experience_site_template_650229.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "Save a template so that you can customize the Experience site for your users. This step is necessary so that you can uninstall the unmanaged package.").
5.  On the splash screen, click **Get Started**.
6.  In the **Name** field, enter the name you want your users to see.
    
    Tip
    
    You can use the same name you used to [Create Your Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_create_your_experience_site_650072.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "To start using the templates provided in the unmanaged package, create an Experience site for your users.").
    
7.  In URL, enter the string you want after the slash. Users will see this URL.
    
    Tip
    
    You can enter the same string that you entered to [Create Your Experience Site](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=ind.insurance_t_create_your_experience_site_650072.htm&language=en_US&type=5 "To start using the templates provided in the unmanaged package, create an Experience site for your users.").
    
8.  Click **Create**.
