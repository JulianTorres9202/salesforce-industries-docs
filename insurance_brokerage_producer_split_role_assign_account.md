# Assign Producer Role to an Account

Assign a role to producers at account level.

### Required Editions

| User Permissions Needed |
| --- |
| To assign producer role to an account: | Insurance Commissions Management and Insurance Brokerage User |

1.  From an account page, on the Producer Role Assignment related list, click **Assign New Role**.
2.  Select the producer, producer role, and effective date.
    
    Line of Business and Expiration Date are optional.
    
3.  To add more producer roles, click Add Row, and then save your changes.
    
    A message confirms that the producer role has been assigned.
