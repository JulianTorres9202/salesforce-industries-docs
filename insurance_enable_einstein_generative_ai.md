# Turn On Einstein Generative AI

To use the Einstein generative AI features to generate a summary from records, turn on the Einstein Generative AI preferences in Setup.

1.  From Setup, in the Quick Find box, enter Einstein Setup, and then select **Einstein Setup**.
2.  Enable Turn on Einstein.
3.  From Setup, in the Quick Find box, enter Insurance Settings, and then select **Insurance Settings**.
4.  Enable Einstein Generative AI for Insurance.
